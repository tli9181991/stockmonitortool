Russia stocks lower at close of trade; MOEX Russia Index down 0.75%
Investing.com – Russia stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Mining, Oil & Gas and Telecoms sectors led shares lower. At the close in Moscow, the MOEX Russia Index fell 0.75%. The worst performers of the session were Moskovskiy Kreditnyi Bank PAO (MCX:CBOM), which fell 5.69% or 0.38 points to trade at 6.25 at the close.
