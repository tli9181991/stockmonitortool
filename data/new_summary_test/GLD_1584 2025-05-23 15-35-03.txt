Turkey stocks lower at close of trade; BIST 100 down 1.26%
Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Sports, Banking and Metal Products & Machinery sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 fell 1.26%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Can2 Termik AS  (IS:CANTE), which rose 5.56% or 0.09 points to trade at 1.71 at the close. Meanwhile,  Pasifik Eurasia Lojistik Dis  Ticaret AS (IS:PASEU) added 3.95% or 2.85 points to end at 74.95 and Baticim Bati Anadolu Cimento Sanayi AS (IS:BTCIM) was up 3.79% or 0.18 points to 4.93 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret  AS (IS:OBAMS), which fell 10.00% or 6.85 points to trade at 61.65 at the close.  Enerya Enerji AS  (IS:ENERY) declined 8.47% or 0.48 points to end at 5.19 and Ard Grup Bilisim Teknolojileri AS (IS:ARDYZ) was down 4.93% or 1.44 points to 27.76. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 403 to 166 and 20 ended unchanged. Shares in Pasifik Eurasia Lojistik Dis Ticaret AS (IS:PASEU) rose to all time highs; gaining 3.95% or 2.85 to 74.95.  Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.94% or 64.06 to $3,359.06 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in July rose 0.85% or 0.52 to hit $61.72 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 0.68% or 0.44 to trade at $64.88 a barrel.
