Investing.com – Canada stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Materials, Energy and Real Estate sectors led shares higher. At the close in Toronto, the S&P/TSX Composite added 0.94%. The best performers of the session on the S&P/TSX Composite were TerraVest Industries Inc (TSX:TVK), which rose 20.54% or 22.33 points to trade at 131.04 at the close. Meanwhile,  B2Gold Corp  (TSX:BTO) added 8.45% or 0.36 points to end at 4.62 and  K92 Mining Inc  (TSX:KNT) was up 6.80% or 0.74 points to 11.62 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Algoma Steel Group Inc (TSX:ASTL), which fell 3.72% or 0.35 points to trade at 9.05 at the close. NFI Group Inc (TSX:NFI) declined 3.43% or 0.46 points to end at 12.96 and Lundin Gold Inc (TSX:LUG) was down 3.05% or 1.35 points to 42.92. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Toronto Stock Exchange by 633 to 287 and 80 ended unchanged. Shares in K92 Mining Inc (TSX:KNT) rose to all time highs; up 6.80% or 0.74 to 11.62.
