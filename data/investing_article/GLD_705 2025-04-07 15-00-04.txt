Investing.com – Norway stocks were lower after the close on Monday, as losses in the Media, Transport and Diversified Financials sectors led shares lower. The worst performers of the session were  Telenor  ASA (OL:TEL), which fell 4.52% or 6.60 points to trade at 139.50 at the close.  Storebrand  ASA (OL:STB) declined 2.86% or 3.50 points to end at 118.90 and  Gjensidige Forsikring ASA  (OL:GJFG) was down 2.77% or 6.40 points to 224.80.
