Investing.com – India stocks were lower after the close on Wednesday, as losses in the Oil & Gas, Real Estate and Public Sector Undertakings sectors led shares lower. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 lost 0.77%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index fell 0.93%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  IndusInd Bank  Ltd. (NSE:INBK), which rose 2.86% or 18.25 points to trade at 655.30 at the close. Meanwhile,  Trent  Ltd (NSE:TREN) added 2.64% or 136.90 points to end at 5,321.95 and  HCL Technologies  Ltd (NSE:HCLT) was up 0.52% or 8.50 points to 1,633.40 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  NTPC  Ltd (NSE:NTPC), which fell 3.35% or 12.30 points to trade at 354.65 at the close.  Tech Mahindra  Ltd (NSE:TEML) declined 2.69% or 39.20 points to end at 1,416.30 and  Cipla  Ltd. (NSE:CIPL) was down 2.32% or 35.00 points to 1,476.20. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were IndusInd Bank Ltd. (BO:INBK) which rose 3.36% to 658.70, Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (BO:PGRD) which was up 0.50% to settle at 292.60 and Titan Company Ltd (BO:TITN) which gained 0.16% to close at 3,060.00. The worst performers were NTPC Ltd (BO:NTPC) which was down 3.45% to 354.50 in late trade, Tech Mahindra Ltd (BO:TEML) which lost 2.97% to settle at 1,413.80 and Eternal Ltd (BO:ZOMT) which was down 2.53% to 204.50 at the close. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1926 to 390 and 136 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2989 fell and 896 advanced, while 101 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 0.66% to 13.55. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.05% or 1.66 to $3,055.96 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 0.84% or 0.58 to hit $69.58 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract rose 0.80% or 0.58 to trade at $72.97 a barrel.
