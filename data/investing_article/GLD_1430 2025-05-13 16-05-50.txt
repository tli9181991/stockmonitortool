Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Technology, Consumer Goods and Oil & Gas sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 rose 0.30% to hit a new 1-month high, while the SBF 120 index added 0.31%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were Stellantis NV (EPA:STLAM), which rose 4.44% or 0.42 points to trade at 9.76 at the close. Meanwhile, STMicroelectronics NV (EPA:STMPA) added 2.87% or 0.65 points to end at 23.31 and  Renault  SA (EPA:RENA) was up 2.53% or 1.21 points to 49.10 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP), which fell 2.38% or 9.40 points to trade at 385.15 at the close.  Edenred  SA (EPA:EDEN) declined 2.02% or 0.57 points to end at 27.66 and Veolia Environnement VE SA (EPA:VIE) was down 1.94% or 0.59 points to 29.77. The top performers on the SBF 120 were Viridien SA (EPA:VIRI) which rose 5.47% to 60.33, Forvia (EPA:FRVIA) which was up 5.34% to settle at 8.52 and  Voltalia SA  (EPA:VLTSA) which gained 4.52% to close at 8.09. The worst performers were Ayvens SA (EPA:AYV) which was down 4.83% to 8.68 in late trade, Emeis SA (EPA:EMEIS) which lost 4.35% to settle at 11.66 and Clariane SE (EPA:CLARI) which was down 3.53% to 3.93 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Paris Stock Exchange by 287 to 208 and 71 ended unchanged. The CAC 40 VIX, which measures the implied volatility of CAC 40 options, was unchanged 0.00% to 18.96 a new 52-week high. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.02% or 32.84 to $3,260.84 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 2.97% or 1.84 to hit $63.79 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 2.65% or 1.72 to trade at $66.68 a barrel.
