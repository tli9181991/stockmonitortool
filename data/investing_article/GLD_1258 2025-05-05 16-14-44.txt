Investing.com – Spain stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Financial Services & Real Estate, Chemical, Petroleum & Plastic and Consumer Services sectors led shares higher. At the close in Madrid, the IBEX 35 gained 0.50% to hit a new 5-year high. The best performers of the session on the IBEX 35 were Corporacion Acciona Energias Renovables SA (BME:ANE), which rose 2.70% or 0.43 points to trade at 16.37 at the close. Meanwhile, SOLARIA ENERGIA Y MEDIO AMBIENTE (BME:SLRS) added 2.61% or 0.17 points to end at 6.53 and  Unicaja Banco SA  (BME:UNI) was up 2.29% or 0.04 points to 1.74 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were ArcelorMittal SA (BME:MTS), which fell 0.59% or 0.16 points to trade at 26.91 at the close.  Puig Brands SA  (BME:PUIGb) declined 0.53% or 0.09 points to end at 16.90 and  Telefonica  (BME:TEF) was down 0.51% or 0.02 points to 4.47. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Madrid Stock Exchange by 135 to 55 and 18 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 2.26% or 73.39 to $3,316.69 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 2.06% or 1.20 to hit $57.09 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.79% or 1.10 to trade at $60.19 a barrel. EUR/USD was unchanged 0.04% to 1.13, while EUR/GBP unchanged 0.05% to 0.85. The US Dollar Index Futures was down 0.09% at 99.75.
