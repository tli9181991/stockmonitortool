Investing.com – Indonesia stocks were lower after the close on Thursday, as losses in the Financials, Agriculture and Basic Industry sectors led shares lower. At the close in Jakarta, the IDX Composite Index fell 0.61%. The best performers of the session on the IDX Composite Index were  Citra Nusantara Gemilang Tbk PT  (JK:CGAS), which rose 34.15% or 28.00 points to trade at 110.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Bank Bukopin  Tbk (JK:BBKP) added 30.77% or 16.00 points to end at 68.00 and  PT Trimuda Nuansa  Citra Tbk (JK:TNCA) was up 30.71% or 39.00 points to 166.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were MDTV Media Technologies Tbk PT (JK:NETV), which fell 14.41% or 34.00 points to trade at 202.00 at the close. Planet Properindo Jaya Pt (JK:PLAN) declined 10.00% or 5.00 points to end at 45.00 and Ricky Putra Globalindo Tbk (JK:RICY) was down 9.76% or 4.00 points to 37.00. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Jakarta Stock Exchange by 327 to 308 and 207 ended unchanged.
