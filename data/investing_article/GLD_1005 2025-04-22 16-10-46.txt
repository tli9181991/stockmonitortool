Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Consumer Goods, Healthcare and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 gained 0.56%, while the SBF 120 index climbed 0.53%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP), which rose 6.30% or 21.55 points to trade at 363.40 at the close. Meanwhile,  Carrefour  SA (EPA:CARR) added 3.45% or 0.46 points to end at 13.66 and  Kering  SA (EPA:PRTP) was up 3.31% or 5.44 points to 169.92 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Schneider Electric SE (EPA:SCHN), which fell 2.78% or 5.70 points to trade at 199.60 at the close.  Legrand  SA (EPA:LEGD) declined 1.64% or 1.54 points to end at 92.12 and Compagnie de Saint Gobain SA (EPA:SGOB) was down 0.76% or 0.66 points to 85.92. The top performers on the SBF 120 were L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP) which rose 6.30% to 363.40, Sartorius  Stedim  Biotech SA (EPA:STDM) which was up 3.93% to settle at 194.50 and  Vivendi  SA (EPA:VIV) which gained 3.83% to close at 2.57. The worst performers were Emeis SA (EPA:EMEIS) which was down 13.87% to 10.00 in late trade, Clariane SE (EPA:CLARI) which lost 12.39% to settle at 3.62 and Eutelsat Communications SA (EPA:ETL) which was down 4.97% to 3.73 at the close. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Paris Stock Exchange by 244 to 236 and 100 ended unchanged. The CAC 40 VIX, which measures the implied volatility of CAC 40 options, was unchanged 0.00% to 18.96 a new 52-week high. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.04% or 1.36 to $3,426.66 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 3.00% or 1.87 to hit $64.28 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract rose 2.54% or 1.68 to trade at $67.94 a barrel. EUR/USD was unchanged 0.43% to 1.15, while EUR/GBP unchanged 0.46% to 0.86. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.32% at 98.36.
