Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Oil & Gas, Auto and Power sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 gained 0.47% to hit a new 3-months high, while the BSE Sensex 30 index gained 0.37%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  Adani Enterprises  Ltd (NSE:ADEL), which rose 7.46% or 171.20 points to trade at 2,465.40 at the close. Meanwhile, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd (NSE:APSE) added 6.31% or 79.90 points to end at 1,347.00 and  Trent  Ltd (NSE:TREN) was up 4.45% or 229.00 points to 5,377.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Kotak Mahindra Bank  Ltd. (NSE:KTKM), which fell 4.56% or 99.70 points to trade at 2,085.50 at the close.  JSW Steel  Ltd (NSE:JSTL) declined 1.85% or 18.00 points to end at 955.20 and Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Ltd (NSE:ONGC) was down 1.49% or 3.62 points to 239.80. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Adani Port and Special Economic Zone Ltd (BO:APSE) which rose 6.31% to 1,347.00, Bajaj Finserv Ltd (BO:BJFS) which was up 3.73% to settle at 2,030.00 and Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. (BO:MAHM) which gained 3.26% to close at 3,026.00. The worst performers were Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. (BO:KTKM) which was down 4.50% to 2,086.65 in late trade, State Bank Of India (BO:SBI) which lost 1.13% to settle at 791.00 and Axis Bank Ltd. (BO:AXBK) which was down 0.64% to 1,174.60 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1656 to 740 and 37 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2440 rose and 1422 declined, while 179 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 0.21% to 18.30. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 2.42% or 78.59 to $3,321.89 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 1.29% or 0.75 to hit $57.54 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.16% or 0.71 to trade at $60.58 a barrel.
