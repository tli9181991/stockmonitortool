Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Chemical, Petroleum & Plastic, Technology and Sports sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 gained 2.25%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were Turkiye Petrol Rafinerileri AS (IS:TUPRS), which rose 9.95% or 11.70 points to trade at 129.30 at the close. Meanwhile,  Aselsan  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS) added 6.87% or 9.90 points to end at 154.10 and Gubre Fabrikalari TAS (IS:GUBRF) was up 5.28% or 14.25 points to 284.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Isiklar Enerji ve Yapi Holding AS (IS:IEYHO), which fell 4.21% or 0.64 points to trade at 14.55 at the close. Grainturk Holding AS (IS:GRTHO) declined 3.01% or 7.60 points to end at 244.90 and  Agrotech Yuksek Teknoloji ve  Yatirim AS (IS:AGROT) was down 2.14% or 0.18 points to 8.24. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 432 to 146 and 15 ended unchanged. Shares in Aselsan Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS) rose to all time highs; gaining 6.87% or 9.90 to 154.10.  Gold Futures for June delivery was down 1.27% or 43.06 to $3,348.84 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 3.05% or 1.77 to hit $59.84 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 2.73% or 1.67 to trade at $62.79 a barrel. USD/TRY was unchanged 0.00% to 38.63, while EUR/TRY rose 0.05% to 43.68. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.60% at 100.03.
