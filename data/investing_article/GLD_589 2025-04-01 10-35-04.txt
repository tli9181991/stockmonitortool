Investing.com – India stocks were lower after the close on Tuesday, as losses in the Real Estate, Consumer Durables and Technology sectors led shares lower. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 fell 1.50%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index declined 1.80%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  IndusInd Bank  Ltd. (NSE:INBK), which rose 4.89% or 31.80 points to trade at 681.65 at the close. Meanwhile,  Trent  Ltd (NSE:TREN) added 4.58% or 243.90 points to end at 5,569.05 and  Bharat Petroleum  Corp. Ltd. (NSE:BPCL) was up 2.60% or 7.23 points to 285.70 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  HCL Technologies  Ltd (NSE:HCLT), which fell 3.63% or 57.85 points to trade at 1,534.65 at the close.  Bharat Electronics  Ltd (NSE:BAJE) declined 3.46% or 10.42 points to end at 290.90 and Bajaj Finserv Ltd (NSE:BJFS) was down 3.36% or 67.35 points to 1,940.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were IndusInd Bank Ltd. (BO:INBK) which rose 5.07% to 682.50, Eternal Ltd (BO:ZOMT) which was up 0.84% to settle at 203.20 and State Bank Of India (BO:SBI) which unchanged 0.00% to close at 771.60. The worst performers were HCL Technologies Ltd (BO:HCLT) which was down 3.66% to 1,532.70 in late trade, Bajaj Finserv Ltd (BO:BJFS) which lost 3.19% to settle at 1,942.05 and HDFC Bank Ltd (BO:HDBK) which was down 3.14% to 1,771.00 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1714 to 701 and 26 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2681 rose and 1210 declined, while 145 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 8.98% to 13.86. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.45% or 14.11 to $3,164.41 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 0.48% or 0.34 to hit $71.82 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract rose 0.45% or 0.34 to trade at $75.11 a barrel.
