Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Banking, Electricity and Holdings & Investments sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 rose 1.33%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Enerya Enerji AS  (IS:ENERY), which rose 9.46% or 0.44 points to trade at 5.09 at the close. Meanwhile,  Ahlatci Dogal Gaz Dagitim Enerji  ve Yatirim AS (IS:AHGAZ) added 9.24% or 2.20 points to end at 26.00 and  Margun Enerji Uretim Sanayi  ve Ticaret AS (IS:MAGEN) was up 8.33% or 2.54 points to 33.04 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Celebi  Hava Servisi AS (IS:CLEBI), which fell 9.98% or 222.00 points to trade at 2,002.00 at the close.  Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret  AS (IS:OBAMS) declined 9.07% or 7.10 points to end at 71.20 and Batisoke Soke Cimento Sanayi TAS (IS:BSOKE) was down 8.61% or 1.78 points to 18.90.
