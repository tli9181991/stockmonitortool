Investing.com – Portugal stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Financials, Industrials and Consumer Goods sectors led shares higher. At the close in Lisbon, the PSI rose 0.36%. The best performers of the session on the PSI were  Mota Engil  SGPS SA (ELI:MOTA), which rose 4.31% or 0.13 points to trade at 3.20 at the close. Meanwhile, Banco Comercial Portugues (ELI:BCP) added 3.60% or 0.02 points to end at 0.54 and Ibersol SGPS (ELI:IBS) was up 3.20% or 0.28 points to 9.04 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Nos SGPS SA (ELI:NOS), which fell 2.80% or 0.13 points to trade at 4.35 at the close. EDP Energias de Portugal SA (ELI:EDP) declined 2.68% or 0.09 points to end at 3.09 and  EDP Renovaveis  (ELI:EDPR) was down 2.25% or 0.20 points to 8.47. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Lisbon Stock Exchange by 14 to 8 and 6 ended unchanged. Shares in Ibersol SGPS (ELI:IBS) rose to 5-year highs; gaining 3.20% or 0.28 to 9.04.  Brent oil for May delivery was up 2.08% or 1.45 to $71.01 a barrel. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in April rose 2.34% or 1.55 to hit $67.80 a barrel, while the April Gold Futures contract rose 0.87% or 25.29 to trade at $2,946.19 a troy ounce.
