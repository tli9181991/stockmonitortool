Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Transport, Telecoms and Wood, Paper & Printing sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 gained 1.20%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were Grainturk Holding AS (IS:GRTHO), which rose 9.94% or 24.35 points to trade at 269.25 at the close. Meanwhile, Oyak Cimento Fabrikalari AS (IS:OYAKC) added 7.09% or 1.52 points to end at 22.96 and TAV Havalimanlari Holding (IS:TAVHL) was up 6.18% or 13.60 points to 233.60 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Otokar  Otomotiv ve Savunma Sanayi AS (IS:OTKAR), which fell 3.31% or 13.25 points to trade at 387.00 at the close.  Aselsan  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS) declined 3.18% or 4.90 points to end at 149.20 and  Katilimevim Tasarruf Finansman AS  (IS:KTLEV) was down 2.88% or 2.65 points to 89.35. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 371 to 199 and 23 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.21% or 39.89 to $3,345.89 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 1.47% or 0.88 to hit $60.79 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 1.42% or 0.89 to trade at $63.73 a barrel.
