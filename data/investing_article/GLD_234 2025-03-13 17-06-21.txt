Investing.com – France stocks were lower after the close on Thursday, as losses in the Technology, Consumer Services and Healthcare sectors led shares lower. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 declined 0.64% to hit a new 1-month low, while the SBF 120 index declined 0.66%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were  Vivendi  SA (EPA:VIV), which rose 1.82% or 0.05 points to trade at 2.86 at the close. Meanwhile,  Teleperformance  SE (EPA:TEPRF) added 1.66% or 1.60 points to end at 97.74 and L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP) was up 1.39% or 4.85 points to 354.85 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Pernod Ricard  SA (EPA:PERP), which fell 3.97% or 3.98 points to trade at 96.22 at the close. EssilorLuxottica SA (EPA:ESLX) declined 2.74% or 7.40 points to end at 262.60 and STMicroelectronics NV (EPA:STMPA) was down 2.71% or 0.62 points to 22.11.
