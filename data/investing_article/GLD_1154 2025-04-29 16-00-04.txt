Investing.com – Finland stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Oil & Gas, Utilities and Consumer Services sectors led shares higher. At the close in Helsinki, the OMX Helsinki 25 gained 0.80%. The best performers of the session on the OMX Helsinki 25 were Neste Oyj (HE:NESTE), which rose 10.34% or 0.82 points to trade at 8.77 at the close. Meanwhile,  Fortum  Oyj (HE:FORTUM) added 7.41% or 1.00 points to end at 14.57 and  Stora Enso  Oyj R (HE:STERV) was up 2.20% or 0.18 points to 8.28 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Kesko  Oyj (HE:KESKOB), which fell 2.14% or 0.44 points to trade at 20.12 at the close. TietoEVRY Corp (HE:TIETO) declined 2.12% or 0.34 points to end at 15.72 and  Qt Group Oyj  (HE:QTCOM) was down 1.66% or 0.95 points to 56.30. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Helsinki Stock Exchange by 92 to 86 and 11 ended unchanged. Shares in Qt Group Oyj (HE:QTCOM) fell to 52-week lows; falling 1.66% or 0.95 to 56.30.  Brent oil for July delivery was down 2.10% or 1.36 to $63.43 a barrel.
