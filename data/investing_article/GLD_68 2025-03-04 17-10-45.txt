Investing.com – Sweden stocks were lower after the close on Tuesday, as losses in the Oil & Gas, Financials and Industrials sectors led shares lower. At the close in Stockholm, the OMX Stockholm 30 declined 3.22%. The best performers of the session on the OMX Stockholm 30 were  Essity AB B  (ST:ESSITYb), which rose 2.20% or 6.60 points to trade at 306.80 at the close. Meanwhile, AstraZeneca PLC (ST:AZN) added 1.12% or 18.00 points to end at 1,625.00 and Telia Company AB (ST:TELIA) was up 0.34% or 0.12 points to 35.11 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Kinnevik, Investment AB ser. B (ST:KINVb), which fell 6.49% or 5.60 points to trade at 80.80 at the close. Electrolux AB Class B (ST:ELUXb) declined 6.22% or 5.70 points to end at 85.70 and AB SKF B (ST:SKFb) was down 5.52% or 12.90 points to 220.80.
