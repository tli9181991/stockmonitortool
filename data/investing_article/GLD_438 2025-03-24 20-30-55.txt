Investing.com – Canada stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the IT, Healthcare and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Toronto, the S&P/TSX Composite rose 1.34%. The worst performers of the session were Lightspeed Commerce Inc (TSX:LSPD), which fell 6.24% or 0.95 points to trade at 14.27 at the close. NovaGold Resources Inc (TSX:NG) declined 4.12% or 0.20 points to end at 4.65 and  Northland Power Inc . (TSX:NPI) was down 3.29% or 0.66 points to 19.40.
