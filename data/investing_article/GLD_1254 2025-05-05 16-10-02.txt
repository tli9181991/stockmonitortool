Investing.com – Belgium stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Consumer Goods, Utilities and Healthcare sectors led shares higher. At the close in Brussels, the BEL 20 rose 0.53% to hit a new 5-year high. Melexis  NV (EBR:MLXS) declined 1.64% or 0.90 points to end at 54.05 and  Umicore  SA (EBR:UMI) was down 0.61% or 0.05 points to 8.15.
