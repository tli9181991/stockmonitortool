Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Real Estate, Metals and Technology sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 gained 3.82% to hit a new 6-months high, while the BSE Sensex 30 index gained 3.74%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  Infosys  Ltd (NSE:INFY), which rose 7.69% or 115.90 points to trade at 1,623.50 at the close. Meanwhile,  Adani Enterprises  Ltd (NSE:ADEL) added 7.60% or 171.00 points to end at 2,422.00 and Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) was up 7.23% or 43.50 points to 644.95 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  IndusInd Bank  Ltd. (NSE:INBK), which fell 3.34% or 27.30 points to trade at 790.90 at the close. Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. (NSE:SUN) declined 3.17% or 55.30 points to end at 1,689.50 and  Bharat Petroleum  Corp. Ltd. (NSE:BPCL) was up 0.67% or 2.05 points to 308.90. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Infosys Ltd (BO:INFY) which rose 7.67% to 1,623.00, HCL Technologies Ltd (BO:HCLT) which was up 5.97% to settle at 1,663.70 and Tata Steel Ltd (BO:TISC) which gained 5.64% to close at 150.80. The worst performers were IndusInd Bank Ltd. (BO:INBK) which was down 3.38% to 790.20 in late trade, Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. (BO:SUN) which lost 3.14% to settle at 1,690.00 and Bharti Airtel Ltd (BO:BRTI) which was up 1.44% to 1,874.90 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 2252 to 168 and 22 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 3394 rose and 568 declined, while 133 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 14.61% to 18.47. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 3.72% or 124.50 to $3,219.50 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 3.15% or 1.92 to hit $62.94 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 2.97% or 1.90 to trade at $65.81 a barrel.
