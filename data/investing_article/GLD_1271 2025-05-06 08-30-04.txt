Investing.com – Indonesia stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Financials, Infrastructure and Agriculture sectors led shares higher. At the close in Jakarta, the IDX Composite Index rose 1.08% to hit a new 1-month high. The best performers of the session on the IDX Composite Index were  Optima Prima Metal Sinergi  (JK:OPMS), which rose 34.92% or 22.00 points to trade at 85.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Informasi Teknologi Indonesia Tbk  (JK:JATI) added 34.44% or 31.00 points to end at 121.00 and  Bangun Karya Perkasa Jaya PT Tbk  (JK:KRYA) was up 32.69% or 34.00 points to 138.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Bersama Zatta Jaya Tbk PT  (JK:ZATA), which fell 12.50% or 1.00 points to trade at 7.00 at the close. Agung Semesta Sejahtera Tbk PT (JK:TARA) declined 11.11% or 1.00 points to end at 8.00 and  Sriwahana  (JK:SWAT) was down 10.00% or 3.00 points to 27.00. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Jakarta Stock Exchange by 316 to 310 and 223 ended unchanged.
