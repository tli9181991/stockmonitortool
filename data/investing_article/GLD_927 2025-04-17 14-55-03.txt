Investing.com – Greece stocks were lower after the close on Thursday, as losses in the Banking, Telecoms and Household sectors led shares lower. At the close in Athens, the Athens General Composite fell 0.79%. The best performers of the session on the Athens General Composite were Elinoil Hellenic Petroleum Company SA (AT:ELIr), which rose 2.94% or 0.06 points to trade at 2.10 at the close. Meanwhile,  Orilina Properties AEEAP  (AT:ORILINAr) added 2.87% or 0.02 points to end at 0.79 and Crete Plastics SA (AT:PLAKR) was up 2.84% or 0.40 points to 14.50 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Premia AE (AT:PREMr), which fell 3.26% or 0.04 points to trade at 1.19 at the close.  Cenergy Holdings SA  (AT:CENEr) declined 3.10% or 0.27 points to end at 8.43 and Eurobank Ergasias SA (AT:EURBr) was down 2.94% or 0.07 points to 2.31.
