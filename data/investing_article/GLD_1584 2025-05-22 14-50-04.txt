Greece stocks lower at close of trade; Athens General Composite down 0.41%
Investing.com – Greece stocks were lower after the close on Thursday, as losses in the Telecoms, Household and Basic Resources sectors led shares lower. At the close in Athens, the Athens General Composite lost 0.41%. The best performers of the session on the Athens General Composite were Metlen Energy & Metals AE (AT:MYTr), which rose 3.07% or 1.36 points to trade at 45.72 at the close. Meanwhile, Elvalhalcor Hellenic Copper and Aluminium Industry SA (AT:ELHA) added 2.03% or 0.05 points to end at 2.27 and Aegean Airlines (AT:AGNr) was up 1.95% or 0.24 points to 12.54 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Orilina Properties AEEAP  (AT:ORILINAr), which fell 6.08% or 0.05 points to trade at 0.77 at the close. Gr Sarantis SA (AT:SRSr) declined 4.00% or 0.56 points to end at 13.44 and  Petros Petropoulos SA  (AT:PTRr) was down 2.97% or 0.28 points to 9.16.
