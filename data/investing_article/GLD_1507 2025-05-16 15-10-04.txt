Investing.com – Morocco stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Utilities, Banking and Mining sectors led shares lower. At the close in Casablanca, the Moroccan All Shares lost 1.25%. The best performers of the session on the Moroccan All Shares were Med Paper (CSE:MDP), which rose 2.26% or 0.50 points to trade at 22.60 at the close. Meanwhile, Maghreb Oxygene (CSE:MOX) added 1.80% or 7.90 points to end at 447.90 and  Afric Industries  Sa (CSE:AFI) was up 1.78% or 6.05 points to 345.95 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Wafa Assurance (CSE:WAA), which fell 4.58% or 235.00 points to trade at 4,900.00 at the close.  Salafin  (CSE:SLF) declined 4.26% or 28.90 points to end at 650.00 and  Colorado  (CSE:COL) was down 3.55% or 2.19 points to 59.50.
