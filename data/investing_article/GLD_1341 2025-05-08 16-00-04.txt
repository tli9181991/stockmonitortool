Investing.com – Finland stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Consumer Services, Basic Materials and Industrials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Helsinki, the OMX Helsinki 25 gained 0.28% to hit a new 1-month high. The best performers of the session on the OMX Helsinki 25 were  Stora Enso  Oyj R (HE:STERV), which rose 3.71% or 0.30 points to trade at 8.38 at the close. Meanwhile, Hiab Oyj (HE:HIAB) added 3.52% or 1.50 points to end at 44.06 and Valmet Oyj (HE:VALMT) was up 3.02% or 0.81 points to 27.61 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Outokumpu Oyj (HE:OUT1V), which fell 4.21% or 0.14 points to trade at 3.23 at the close. Orion Oyj B (HE:ORNBV) declined 2.67% or 1.50 points to end at 54.75 and Elisa Oyj (HE:ELISA) was down 2.03% or 0.96 points to 46.26. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Helsinki Stock Exchange by 87 to 82 and 19 ended unchanged. Brent oil for July delivery was up 2.88% or 1.76 to $62.88 a barrel. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 3.24% or 1.88 to hit $59.95 a barrel, while the June Gold Futures contract fell 1.69% or 57.26 to trade at $3,334.64 a troy ounce.
