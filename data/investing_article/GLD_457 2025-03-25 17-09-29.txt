Investing.com – Portugal stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Financials, Consumer Services and Industrials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Lisbon, the PSI added 0.86%. The best performers of the session on the PSI were Banco Comercial Portugues (ELI:BCP), which rose 3.88% or 0.02 points to trade at 0.57 at the close. The worst performers of the session were Altri SGPS SA (ELI:ALSS), which fell 1.14% or 0.07 points to trade at 6.05 at the close.  EDP Renovaveis  (ELI:EDPR) declined 0.94% or 0.08 points to end at 7.91 and EDP Energias de Portugal SA (ELI:EDP) was down 0.32% or 0.01 points to 3.09.
