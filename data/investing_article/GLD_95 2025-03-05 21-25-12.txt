Investing.com – Russia stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Telecoms, Power and Oil & Gas sectors led shares higher. At the close in Moscow, the MOEX Russia Index gained 0.83%. The best performers of the session on the MOEX Russia Index were  PhosAgro  PJSC (MCX:PHOR), which rose 0.80% or 54.00 points to trade at 6,798.00 at the close. Meanwhile, Unipro PJSC (MCX:UPRO) added 0.54% or 0.01 points to end at 2.61 and PIK SHb PJSC (MCX:PIKK) was up 0.41% or 2.70 points to 668.90 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were MMC NORILSK NICKEL PJSC (MCX:GMKN), which fell 2.94% or 4.10 points to trade at 136.80 at the close. Bank VTB PJSC (MCX:VTBR) declined 2.89% or 2.69 points to end at 90.49 and OK Rusal MKPAO (MCX:RUAL) was down 2.83% or 1.18 points to 40.54. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Moscow Stock Exchange by 127 to 117 and 10 ended unchanged. The Russian Volatility Index – RVI, which measures the implied volatility of MOEX Russia Index options, was up 7.08% to 53.72 a new 3-months high. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.29% or 8.34 to $2,928.94 a troy ounce.
