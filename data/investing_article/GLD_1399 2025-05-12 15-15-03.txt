Investing.com – Morocco stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Banking, Beverage and Transport sectors led shares higher. At the close in  Casablanca , the Moroccan All Shares added 1.51% to hit a new all time high. The best performers of the session on the Moroccan All Shares were Marocaine Ste de Therapeutique SA (CSE:SOT), which rose 8.99% or 117.00 points to trade at 1,419.00 at the close. Meanwhile, Taqa Morocco SA (CSE:TQM) added 6.30% or 160.00 points to end at 2,700.00 and Travaux Generaux De Construction De Casablanca S.A (CSE:TGC) was up 5.32% or 41.00 points to 811.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Med Paper (CSE:MDP), which fell 3.59% or 0.82 points to trade at 22.00 at the close. SMI (CSE:SMI) declined 1.96% or 47.00 points to end at 2,350.00 and Fenie Brossette (CSE:FBR) was down 1.82% or 5.00 points to 270.00. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Casablanca Stock Exchange by 39 to 16 and 5 ended unchanged.
