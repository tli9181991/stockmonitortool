Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Industrials, Financials and Basic Materials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 gained 1.10%, while the SBF 120 index climbed 1.06%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were Airbus Group SE (EPA:AIR), which rose 3.24% or 5.28 points to trade at 168.06 at the close. Meanwhile, L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP) added 2.99% or 10.25 points to end at 353.10 and Schneider Electric SE (EPA:SCHN) was up 2.44% or 5.15 points to 215.90 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Eurofins Scientific SE (EPA:EUFI), which fell 1.18% or 0.58 points to trade at 48.64 at the close. Bureau Veritas SA (EPA:BVI) declined 0.29% or 0.08 points to end at 27.86 and TotalEnergies SE (EPA:TTEF) was down 0.28% or 0.17 points to 59.49. The top performers on the SBF 120 were Solutions 30 SE (EPA:S30) which rose 10.74% to 1.69,  Worldline SA  (EPA:WLN) which was up 5.27% to settle at 5.96 and  Lectra  SA (EPA:LECS) which gained 4.15% to close at 27.60. The worst performers were Eutelsat Communications SA (EPA:ETL) which was down 9.30% to 3.98 in late trade,  Ubisoft  Entertainment (EPA:UBIP) which lost 3.14% to settle at 10.81 and  Voltalia SA  (EPA:VLTSA) which was down 2.70% to 6.49 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Paris Stock Exchange by 284 to 215 and 76 ended unchanged. Shares in Voltalia SA (EPA:VLTSA) fell to 52-week lows; losing 2.70% or 0.18 to 6.49.  The CAC 40 VIX, which measures the implied volatility of CAC 40 options, was unchanged 0.00% to 18.96 a new 52-week high. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 0.20% or 6.39 to $3,143.91 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May fell 0.17% or 0.12 to hit $71.36 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract fell 0.15% or 0.11 to trade at $74.66 a barrel. EUR/USD was unchanged 0.14% to 1.08, while EUR/GBP unchanged 0.16% to 0.84. The US Dollar Index Futures was down 0.06% at 103.82.
