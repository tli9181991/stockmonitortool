Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Power, Metals and Public Sector Undertakings sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 rose 1.19%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index climbed 1.01%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd (NSE:APSE), which rose 5.18% or 54.75 points to trade at 1,112.70 at the close. Meanwhile,  Tata Steel  Ltd (NSE:TISC) added 4.85% or 6.76 points to end at 146.10 and  Adani Enterprises  Ltd (NSE:ADEL) was up 4.71% or 101.05 points to 2,245.85 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Bajaj Finance  Ltd (NSE:BJFN), which fell 3.35% or 287.25 points to trade at 8,298.70 at the close.  IndusInd Bank  Ltd. (NSE:INBK) declined 1.59% or 15.75 points to end at 971.85 and  HDFC Bank Ltd  (NSE:HDBK) was down 1.17% or 20.00 points to 1,690.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Adani Port and Special Economic Zone Ltd (BO:APSE) which rose 4.97% to 1,111.95, Tata Steel Ltd (BO:TISC) which was up 4.63% to settle at 145.75 and Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (BO:PGRD) which gained 4.31% to close at 265.00. The worst performers were Bajaj Finance Ltd (BO:BJFN) which was down 3.49% to 8,288.95 in late trade, IndusInd Bank Ltd. (BO:INBK) which lost 1.60% to settle at 972.10 and HDFC Bank Ltd (BO:HDBK) which was down 1.07% to 1,692.80 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 2058 to 286 and 84 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 3089 rose and 756 declined, while 88 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 0.74% to 13.73. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.24% or 7.04 to $2,927.64 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in April fell 1.49% or 1.02 to hit $67.24 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract fell 1.01% or 0.72 to trade at $70.32 a barrel.
