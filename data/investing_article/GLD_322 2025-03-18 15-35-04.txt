Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Tuesday, as losses in the Banking, Transport and Wholesale & Retail Trade sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 lost 0.55%. Net Holding  AS (IS:NTHOL) declined 2.94% or 1.32 points to end at 43.52 and  Petkim  Petrokimya Holding AS (IS:PETKM) was down 2.93% or 0.54 points to 17.87.
