Germany stocks lower at close of trade; DAX down 0.47%
Investing.com – Germany stocks were lower after the close on Thursday, as losses in the Media, Construction and Consumer & Cyclical sectors led shares lower. At the close in Frankfurt, the DAX fell 0.47%, while the MDAX index fell 1.27%, and the TecDAX index declined 1.03%. The best performers of the session on the DAX were  Siemens Energy AG  (ETR:ENR1n), which rose 3.18% or 2.52 points to trade at 81.68 at the close. Meanwhile,  Bayer AG  NA (ETR:BAYGN) added 2.72% or 0.65 points to end at 24.36 and  Commerzbank AG  O.N. (ETR:CBKG) was up 1.29% or 0.34 points to 26.63 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Dr Ing hc F Porsche AG Preferred (ETR:P911_p), which fell 4.31% or 1.98 points to trade at 44.00 at the close. Merck KGaA (ETR:MRCG) declined 2.93% or 3.45 points to end at 114.30 and Heidelberg Materials AG (ETR:HEIG) was down 2.41% or 4.40 points to 177.95.
