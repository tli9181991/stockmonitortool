Investing.com – Belgium stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Industrials, Healthcare and Basic Materials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Brussels, the BEL 20 rose 0.37%. Meanwhile, UCB SA (EBR:UCB) added 2.57% or 3.75 points to end at 149.70 and  Umicore  SA (EBR:UMI) was up 2.14% or 0.18 points to 8.36 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Lotus Bakeries  (EBR:LOTB), which fell 1.68% or 140.00 points to trade at 8,190.00 at the close. Anheuser Busch Inbev SA NV (EBR:ABI) declined 1.58% or 0.92 points to end at 57.32 and Elia (EBR:ELI) was down 0.72% or 0.65 points to 89.50. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Brussels Stock Exchange by 56 to 38 and 8 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 1.61% or 53.90 to $3,294.70 a troy ounce.
