Investing.com – Norway stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Healthcare Equipment & Services, Pharma Biotech & Life Sciences and Utilities sectors led shares higher. At the close in Oslo, the Oslo OBX added 0.47% to hit a new all time high. The best performers of the session on the Oslo OBX were TGS NOPEC Geophysical Company ASA (OL:TGS), which rose 4.04% or 4.00 points to trade at 103.00 at the close. Meanwhile, Aker BP ASA (OL:AKRBP) added 3.04% or 7.10 points to end at 241.00 and Nel ASA (OL:NEL) was up 2.63% or 0.08 points to 3.28 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Kongsberg Gruppen ASA  (OL:KOG), which fell 4.06% or 68.00 points to trade at 1,606.00 at the close.  Golden Ocean  Group Ltd (OL:GOGL) declined 1.84% or 1.52 points to end at 80.88 and Orkla ASA (OL:ORK) was down 0.18% or 0.20 points to 109.60. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Oslo Stock Exchange by 180 to 87 and 37 ended unchanged.
