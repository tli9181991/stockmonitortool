Investing.com – Germany stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Technology, Consumer & Cyclical and Food & Beverages sectors led shares higher. At the close in Frankfurt, the DAX gained 0.49%, while the MDAX index gained 0.92%, and the TecDAX index added 1.15%. The best performers of the session on the DAX were Infineon Technologies AG NA O.N. (ETR:IFXGn), which rose 7.08% or 1.96 points to trade at 29.58 at the close. Meanwhile, Adidas AG (ETR:ADSGN) added 2.52% or 5.30 points to end at 215.60 and  Siemens  AG Class N (ETR:SIEGn) was up 2.11% or 4.15 points to 200.65 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Commerzbank AG  O.N. (ETR:CBKG), which fell 2.75% or 0.66 points to trade at 23.35 at the close.  Brenntag AG  (ETR:BNRGn) declined 1.62% or 0.92 points to end at 55.74 and Munich Reinsurance (ETR:MUVGn) was down 1.24% or 7.60 points to 603.00. The top performers on the MDAX were  Aixtron  SE (ETR:AIXGn) which rose 4.38% to 11.08, Redcare Pharmacy NV (ETR:RDC) which was up 4.22% to settle at 128.40 and  RENK  Group AG (ETR:R3NK) which gained 3.19% to close at 48.99. The worst performers were  Nordex  SE O.N. (ETR:NDXG) which was down 1.38% to 15.76 in late trade, Stroeer SE (ETR:SAXG) which lost 1.33% to settle at 51.80 and Deutsche Lufthansa AG (ETR:LHAG) which was down 1.18% to 6.36 at the close. The top performers on the TecDAX were Infineon Technologies AG NA O.N. (ETR:IFXGn) which rose 7.08% to 29.58, Elmos Semiconductor AG (ETR:ELGG) which was up 5.40% to settle at 58.60 and Aixtron SE (ETR:AIXGn) which gained 4.38% to close at 11.08. The worst performers were  Nagarro SE  (ETR:NA9n) which was down 1.41% to 66.65 in late trade, Nordex SE O.N. (ETR:NDXG) which lost 1.38% to settle at 15.76 and Deutsche Telekom AG Na (ETR:DTEGn) which was down 0.40% to 32.75 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange by 397 to 221 and 24 ended unchanged. The DAX volatility index, which measures the implied volatility of DAX options, was down 4.93% to 24.88. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.49% or 49.24 to $3,343.34 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 0.05% or 0.03 to hit $62.24 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract fell 0.14% or 0.09 to trade at $66.03 a barrel.
