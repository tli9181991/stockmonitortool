Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Wednesday, as losses in the Leasing & Factoring, Sports and Textile & Leather sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 declined 1.36%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret  AS (IS:OBAMS), which rose 5.31% or 3.35 points to trade at 66.45 at the close. Meanwhile,  Aselsan  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS) added 3.95% or 4.60 points to end at 121.20 and Ral Yatirim Holding AS (IS:RALYH) was up 3.55% or 3.70 points to 108.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Margun Enerji Uretim Sanayi  ve Ticaret AS (IS:MAGEN), which fell 4.79% or 1.40 points to trade at 27.80 at the close.  Karsan Otomotiv  Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:KARSN) declined 4.76% or 0.51 points to end at 10.20 and  Pegasus Hava Tasimaciligi  AS (IS:PGSUS) was down 4.47% or 11.75 points to 251.00. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 507 to 83 and 2 ended unchanged. Shares in Ral Yatirim Holding AS (IS:RALYH) rose to all time highs; up 3.55% or 3.70 to 108.00.  Gold Futures for June delivery was up 2.85% or 92.21 to $3,332.61 a troy ounce.
