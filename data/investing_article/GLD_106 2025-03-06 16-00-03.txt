Investing.com – Norway stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Healthcare Equipment & Services, Pharma Biotech & Life Sciences and Utilities sectors led shares higher. At the close in Oslo, the Oslo OBX gained 0.40%. The best performers of the session on the Oslo OBX were Norsk Hydro ASA (OL:NHY), which rose 6.94% or 4.64 points to trade at 71.48 at the close. The worst performers of the session were Nel ASA (OL:NEL), which fell 6.28% or 0.13 points to trade at 1.99 at the close. TGS NOPEC Geophysical Company ASA (OL:TGS) declined 3.29% or 3.40 points to end at 99.90 and  Golden Ocean  Group Ltd (OL:GOGL) was down 3.18% or 3.04 points to 92.56.
