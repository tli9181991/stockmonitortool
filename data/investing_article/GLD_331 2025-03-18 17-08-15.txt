Investing.com – Italy stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Industrials, Financials and Technology sectors led shares higher. At the close in Milan, the Investing.com Italy 40 added 1.20% to hit a new 5-year high. The best performers of the session on the Investing.com Italy 40 were Iveco Group NV (BIT:IVG), which rose 5.20% or 0.84 points to trade at 17.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Prysmian  SpA (BIT:PRY) added 3.62% or 2.08 points to end at 59.48 and  Unipol  Gruppo Finanziario Spa (BIT:UNPI) was up 2.61% or 0.39 points to 15.32 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Telecom Italia  (BIT:TLIT), which fell 1.85% or 0.01 points to trade at 0.29 at the close.  Ferrari  NV (BIT:RACE) declined 1.30% or 5.40 points to end at 410.10 and  Terna  Rete Elettrica Nazionale SpA (BIT:TRN) was down 1.08% or 0.09 points to 7.91.
