Investing.com – Russia stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Telecoms, Mining and Oil & Gas sectors led shares higher. At the close in Moscow, the MOEX Russia Index rose 1.15%. The best performers of the session on the MOEX Russia Index were AFK Sistema PJSC (MCX:AFKS), which rose 3.06% or 0.55 points to trade at 18.53 at the close. Meanwhile, Transneft PJSC Pref (MCX:TRNF_p) added 2.33% or 27.00 points to end at 1,168.00 and Moskovskiy Kreditnyi Bank PAO (MCX:CBOM) was up 2.24% or 0.22 points to 9.95 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Polyus PJSC (MCX:PLZL), which fell 2.72% or 528.50 points to trade at 18,871.00 at the close. Federal Hydro Generating Company RusHydro PJSC (MCX:HYDR) declined 2.38% or 0.01 points to end at 0.52 and Magnit PJSC (MCX:MGNT) was down 1.92% or 95.00 points to 4,841.50. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Moscow Stock Exchange by 158 to 83 and 11 ended unchanged. The Russian Volatility Index – RVI, which measures the implied volatility of MOEX Russia Index options, was up 5.69% to 49.96. Gold Futures for April delivery was down 0.71% or 20.80 to $2,893.30 a troy ounce.
