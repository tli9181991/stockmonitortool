Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Metals, Auto and Banking sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 added 0.54%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index gained 0.46%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were Dr Reddy’s Laboratories Ltd (NSE:REDY), which rose 3.86% or 42.75 points to trade at 1,150.70 at the close. Meanwhile, Bajaj Finserv Ltd (NSE:BJFS) added 3.55% or 64.15 points to end at 1,871.60 and  SBI Life Insurance  Company Ltd (NSE:SBIL) was up 3.51% or 48.70 points to 1,434.25 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Wipro Ltd  (NSE:WIPR), which fell 1.57% or 4.15 points to trade at 259.85 at the close.  Britannia Industries  Ltd (NSE:BRIT) declined 1.13% or 53.35 points to end at 4,674.90 and  Bharat Petroleum  Corp. Ltd. (NSE:BPCL) was down 1.13% or 2.99 points to 261.42. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Bajaj Finserv Ltd (BO:BJFS) which rose 3.77% to 1,875.00, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. (BO:MAHM) which was up 2.41% to settle at 2,707.00 and Axis Bank Ltd. (BO:AXBK) which gained 2.31% to close at 1,033.50. The worst performers were ITC Ltd (BO:ITC) which was down 1.03% to 407.60 in late trade, Nestle India Ltd (BO:NEST) which lost 0.76% to settle at 2,175.30 and State Bank Of India (BO:SBI) which was down 0.54% to 723.80 at the close. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1592 to 773 and 100 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2461 fell and 1492 advanced, while 116 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 1.04% to 13.42. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.06% or 1.69 to $3,002.79 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 0.97% or 0.65 to hit $67.56 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract rose 0.95% or 0.67 to trade at $71.25 a barrel. USD/INR was down 0.10% to 86.86, while EUR/INR fell 0.02% to 94.70. The US Dollar Index Futures was down 0.22% at 103.14.
