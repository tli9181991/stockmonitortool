Investing.com – Germany stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Industrials, Technology and Software sectors led shares higher. At the close in Frankfurt, the DAX rose 2.49% to hit a new 1-month high, while the MDAX index added 1.85%, and the TecDAX index gained 2.47%. The best performers of the session on the DAX were  Siemens Energy AG  (ETR:ENR1n), which rose 7.49% or 5.06 points to trade at 72.60 at the close. Meanwhile,  Airbus Group SE  (ETR:AIRG) added 5.33% or 7.84 points to end at 154.94 and  Rheinmetall  AG (ETR:RHMG) was up 5.14% or 77.00 points to 1,576.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Munich Reinsurance (ETR:MUVGn), which fell 3.72% or 22.40 points to trade at 579.20 at the close. RWE AG ST O.N. (ETR:RWEG) declined 3.07% or 1.05 points to end at 33.15 and BASF SE NA O.N. (ETR:BASFN) was down 0.54% or 0.24 points to 44.39. The top performers on the MDAX were  Aixtron  SE (ETR:AIXGn) which rose 9.35% to 12.92,  United Internet  AG NA (ETR:UTDI) which was up 5.58% to settle at 21.00 and  Delivery Hero AG  (ETR:DHER) which gained 4.88% to close at 26.00. The worst performers were  RTL Group SA  (ETR:RRTL) which was down 10.00% to 32.40 in late trade,  TeamViewer  AG (ETR:TMV) which lost 1.11% to settle at 13.32 and  Aroundtown Property Holdings  PLC (ETR:AT1) which was down 0.53% to 2.61 at the close. The top performers on the TecDAX were Aixtron SE (ETR:AIXGn) which rose 9.35% to 12.92, IONOS Group SE (ETR:IOSn) which was up 6.48% to settle at 31.20 and  Siltronic AG  (ETR:WAFGn) which gained 6.29% to close at 37.84. The worst performers were  PNE Wind AG  (ETR:PNEGn) which was down 1.95% to 15.10 in late trade, TeamViewer AG (ETR:TMV) which lost 1.11% to settle at 13.32 and Carl Zeiss Meditec AG (ETR:AFXG) which was down 0.50% to 59.80 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange by 478 to 155 and 23 ended unchanged. Shares in Siemens Energy AG (ETR:ENR1n) rose to all time highs; up 7.49% or 5.06 to 72.60. Shares in Rheinmetall AG (ETR:RHMG) rose to all time highs; up 5.14% or 77.00 to 1,576.00. Shares in IONOS Group SE (ETR:IOSn) rose to all time highs; rising 6.48% or 1.90 to 31.20.  The DAX volatility index, which measures the implied volatility of DAX options, was up 4.60% to 23.40. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.43% or 13.99 to $3,236.19 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 1.54% or 0.91 to hit $58.33 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.27% or 0.79 to trade at $61.34 a barrel.
