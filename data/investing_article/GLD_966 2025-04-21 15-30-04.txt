Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Metal Products & Machinery, Tourism and Non-Metal Mineral Products sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 gained 0.05%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were Tofas Turk Otomobil Fabrikasi AS (IS:TOASO), which rose 9.97% or 17.60 points to trade at 194.10 at the close. Meanwhile,  Galatasaray Sportif  Sinai ve Ticari Yatirimlar AS (IS:GSRAY) added 9.63% or 0.18 points to end at 2.05 and Isiklar Enerji ve Yapi Holding AS (IS:IEYHO) was up 8.15% or 0.97 points to 12.87 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Grainturk Holding AS (IS:GRTHO), which fell 9.90% or 38.25 points to trade at 348.00 at the close.  Mia Teknoloji AS  (IS:MIATK) declined 8.17% or 3.24 points to end at 36.44 and  Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret  AS (IS:OBAMS) was down 5.48% or 4.00 points to 69.00. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 341 to 228 and 26 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 3.14% or 104.49 to $3,432.89 a troy ounce.
