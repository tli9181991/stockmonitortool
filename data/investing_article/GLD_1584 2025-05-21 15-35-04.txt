Turkey stocks lower at close of trade; BIST 100 down 1.21%
Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Wednesday, as losses in the Sports, Transport and Basic Metals sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 declined 1.21%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Enerya Enerji AS  (IS:ENERY), which rose 6.39% or 0.32 points to trade at 5.33 at the close. Meanwhile, SASA Polyester Sanayi AS (IS:SASA) added 3.95% or 0.14 points to end at 3.68 and  Avrupakent Gayrimenkul Yatirim  Ortakligi AS (IS:AVPGY) was up 1.87% or 1.00 points to 54.50 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Vestel  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:VESTL), which fell 5.68% or 2.34 points to trade at 38.88 at the close.  Celebi  Hava Servisi AS (IS:CLEBI) declined 4.48% or 91.00 points to end at 1,941.00 and Turkiye Halk Bankasi (IS:HALKB) was down 4.09% or 0.88 points to 20.66. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 399 to 176 and 16 ended unchanged.
