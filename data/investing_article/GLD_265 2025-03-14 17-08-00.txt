Investing.com – Italy stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Industrials, Telecoms and Technology sectors led shares higher. At the close in Milan, the Investing.com Italy 40 added 1.69%. The best performers of the session on the Investing.com Italy 40 were Iveco Group NV (BIT:IVG), which rose 7.18% or 1.09 points to trade at 16.20 at the close. Meanwhile, Leonardo SpA (BIT:LDOF) added 7.13% or 3.14 points to end at 47.20 and  Buzzi Unicem  (BIT:BZU) was up 7.00% or 3.50 points to 53.50 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  DiaSorin SpA  (BIT:DIAS), which fell 2.67% or 2.62 points to trade at 95.44 at the close. Snam SpA (BIT:SRG) declined 1.39% or 0.06 points to end at 4.53 and Nexi SpA (BIT:NEXII) was down 1.07% or 0.06 points to 5.17.
