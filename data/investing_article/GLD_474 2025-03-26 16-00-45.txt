Investing.com – Norway stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Healthcare Equipment & Services, Pharma Biotech & Life Sciences and Utilities sectors led shares higher. At the close in Oslo, the Oslo OBX added 0.67%. The best performers of the session on the Oslo OBX were TGS NOPEC Geophysical Company ASA (OL:TGS), which rose 2.92% or 3.00 points to trade at 105.60 at the close. Meanwhile,  Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA  (OL:NAS) added 2.60% or 0.31 points to end at 12.22 and Aker BP ASA (OL:AKRBP) was up 2.15% or 5.20 points to 247.30 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Nel ASA (OL:NEL), which fell 7.56% or 0.21 points to trade at 2.54 at the close.  Frontline Ltd  (OL:FRO) declined 1.77% or 3.00 points to end at 166.45 and  Nordic Semiconductor ASA  (OL:NOD) was down 1.39% or 1.85 points to 131.05. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Oslo Stock Exchange by 144 to 129 and 24 ended unchanged. Crude oil for May delivery was up 1.45% or 1.00 to $70.00 a barrel.
