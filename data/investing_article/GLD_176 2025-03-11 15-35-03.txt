Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Leasing & Factoring, Technology and Non-Metal Mineral Products sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 added 0.15%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Ulker Biskuvi  Sanayi AS (IS:ULKER), which rose 10.00% or 12.30 points to trade at 135.30 at the close. Meanwhile,  Tukas  Gida Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:TUKAS) added 7.54% or 0.15 points to end at 2.14 and  Cimsa  Cimento Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:CIMSA) was up 7.48% or 3.60 points to 51.70 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Isiklar Enerji ve Yapi Holding AS (IS:IEYHO), which fell 7.55% or 0.73 points to trade at 8.94 at the close.  Vestel  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:VESTL) declined 4.77% or 2.90 points to end at 57.95 and  Girisim Elektrik Taahhut  Ticaret & Sanayi AS (IS:GESAN) was down 3.89% or 2.10 points to 51.90. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 297 to 280 and 15 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.83% or 24.09 to $2,923.49 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in April rose 1.30% or 0.86 to hit $66.89 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract rose 1.28% or 0.89 to trade at $70.17 a barrel.
