Investing.com – Belgium stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Financials, Industrials and Healthcare sectors led shares higher. At the close in Brussels, the BEL 20 rose 1.44% to hit a new 1-month high. The best performers of the session on the BEL 20 were  Cofinimmo  SA (EBR:COFB), which rose 10.43% or 7.35 points to trade at 77.85 at the close. The worst performers of the session were  Aedifica  (EBR:AOO), which fell 2.90% or 2.05 points to trade at 68.60 at the close. Elia (EBR:ELI) declined 2.51% or 2.40 points to end at 93.20 and Groep Brussel Lambert NV (EBR:GBLB) was down 1.52% or 1.10 points to 71.40.
