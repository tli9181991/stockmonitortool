Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Industrials, Oil & Gas and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 gained 0.59%, while the SBF 120 index climbed 0.61%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were  Safran  SA (EPA:SAF), which rose 4.99% or 11.90 points to trade at 250.50 at the close. Meanwhile, Schneider Electric SE (EPA:SCHN) added 2.27% or 5.05 points to end at 227.80 and AXA SA (EPA:AXAF) was up 2.08% or 0.79 points to 38.82 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Edenred  SA (EPA:EDEN), which fell 4.83% or 1.63 points to trade at 32.14 at the close.  Teleperformance  SE (EPA:TEPRF) declined 3.22% or 3.20 points to end at 96.14 and L’Oreal SA (EPA:OREP) was down 1.75% or 6.25 points to 350.00. The top performers on the SBF 120 were Clariane SE (EPA:CLARI) which rose 14.42% to 4.22, Atos SE (EPA:ATOS) which was unchanged 10.81% to settle at 0.00 and Safran SA (EPA:SAF) which gained 4.99% to close at 250.50. The worst performers were Eutelsat Communications SA (EPA:ETL) which was down 9.45% to 6.80 in late trade,  Remy Cointreau  (EPA:RCOP) which lost 5.01% to settle at 47.06 and Edenred SA (EPA:EDEN) which was down 4.83% to 32.14 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Paris Stock Exchange by 269 to 214 and 88 ended unchanged. Shares in AXA SA (EPA:AXAF) rose to 5-year highs; up 2.08% or 0.79 to 38.82. Shares in Remy Cointreau (EPA:RCOP) fell to 5-year lows; losing 5.01% or 2.48 to 47.06.  The CAC 40 VIX, which measures the implied volatility of CAC 40 options, was unchanged 0.00% to 18.96 a new 52-week high. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.85% or 24.79 to $2,945.69 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in April rose 2.25% or 1.49 to hit $67.74 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract rose 1.98% or 1.38 to trade at $70.94 a barrel.
