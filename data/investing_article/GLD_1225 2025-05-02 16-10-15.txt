Investing.com – Spain stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Consumer Services, Building & Construction and Financial Services & Real Estate sectors led shares higher. At the close in Madrid, the IBEX 35 gained 1.01%. The best performers of the session on the IBEX 35 were International Consolidated Airlines Group SA (BME:ICAG), which rose 6.78% or 0.21 points to trade at 3.27 at the close. Meanwhile, ArcelorMittal SA (BME:MTS) added 4.96% or 1.28 points to end at 27.09 and Indra A (BME:IDR) was up 4.43% or 1.24 points to 29.26 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were SOLARIA ENERGIA Y MEDIO AMBIENTE (BME:SLRS), which fell 4.04% or 0.27 points to trade at 6.37 at the close. Redeia Corporacion SA (BME:REDE) declined 3.68% or 0.68 points to end at 17.82 and Corporacion Acciona Energias Renovables SA (BME:ANE) was down 3.04% or 0.50 points to 15.94.
