Investing.com – Denmark stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Oil & Gas, Real Estate and Chemicals sectors led shares lower. The worst performers of the session were  Zealand Pharma  A/S (CSE:ZELA), which fell 2.73% or 15.00 points to trade at 533.50 at the close.
