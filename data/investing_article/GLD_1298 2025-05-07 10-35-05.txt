Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Auto, Metals and Real Estate sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 added 0.14%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index climbed 0.13%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  Tata Motors Ltd  (NSE:TAMO), which rose 5.14% or 33.30 points to trade at 681.50 at the close. Meanwhile,  Bajaj Finance  Ltd (NSE:BJFN) added 1.98% or 174.50 points to end at 8,972.00 and Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) was up 1.83% or 11.40 points to 634.85 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Asian Paints  Ltd. (NSE:ASPN), which fell 3.97% or 96.00 points to trade at 2,322.00 at the close. Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. (NSE:SUN) declined 2.10% or 38.20 points to end at 1,780.90 and  Bajaj Auto  Ltd (NSE:BAJA) was down 1.29% or 102.50 points to 7,843.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Tata Motors Ltd (BO:TAMO) which rose 5.20% to 681.50, Bajaj Finance Ltd (BO:BJFN) which was up 2.02% to settle at 8,975.00 and Eternal Ltd (BO:ETEA) which gained 1.63% to close at 236.40. The worst performers were Asian Paints Ltd. (BO:ASPN) which was down 4.00% to 2,321.35 in late trade, Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. (BO:SUN) which lost 1.95% to settle at 1,783.35 and ITC Ltd (BO:ITC) which was down 1.30% to 430.00 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1421 to 929 and 34 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2103 rose and 1630 declined, while 152 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 0.04% to 19.00. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 0.73% or 25.04 to $3,397.76 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 0.49% or 0.29 to hit $59.38 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 0.29% or 0.18 to trade at $62.33 a barrel. USD/INR was up 0.57% to 84.77, while EUR/INR rose 0.43% to 96.27. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.25% at 99.29.
