Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Financials, Utilities and Industrials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 gained 0.50%, while the SBF 120 index gained 0.52%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were  BNP Paribas  SA (EPA:BNPP), which rose 2.95% or 2.30 points to trade at 80.21 at the close. Meanwhile,  Societe Generale  SA (EPA:SOGN) added 2.48% or 1.03 points to end at 42.75 and STMicroelectronics NV (EPA:STMPA) was up 2.40% or 0.55 points to 23.48 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Vivendi  SA (EPA:VIV), which fell 2.66% or 0.07 points to trade at 2.71 at the close.  Hermes International  SCA (EPA:HRMS) declined 1.61% or 40.00 points to end at 2,446.00 and  Danone  SA (EPA:DANO) was down 1.36% or 0.98 points to 70.82.
