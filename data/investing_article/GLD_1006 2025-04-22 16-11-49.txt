Investing.com – Germany stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Chemicals, Consumer & Cyclical and Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare sectors led shares higher. At the close in Frankfurt, the DAX gained 0.34%, while the MDAX index gained 0.39%, and the TecDAX index gained 0.16%. The best performers of the session on the DAX were Sartorius AG VZO O.N. (ETR:SATG_p), which rose 5.45% or 11.40 points to trade at 220.40 at the close. Meanwhile, Volkswagen AG VZO O.N. (ETR:VOWG_p) added 3.19% or 2.86 points to end at 92.40 and  Zalando SE  (ETR:ZALG) was up 3.04% or 1.04 points to 35.29 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were SAP SE (ETR:SAPG), which fell 3.32% or 7.50 points to trade at 218.50 at the close.  Rheinmetall  AG (ETR:RHMG) declined 2.60% or 38.00 points to end at 1,426.00 and  Airbus Group SE  (ETR:AIRG) was down 2.48% or 3.38 points to 133.12. The worst performers were  Aroundtown Property Holdings  PLC (ETR:AT1) which was down 1.69% to 2.67 in late trade,  TeamViewer  AG (ETR:TMV) which lost 1.35% to settle at 12.40 and Redcare Pharmacy NV (ETR:RDC) which was down 1.34% to 124.80 at the close.
