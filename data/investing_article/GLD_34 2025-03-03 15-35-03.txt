Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Banking, Wholesale & Retail Trade and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 rose 2.61%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Dogus Otomotiv  Servis ve Ticaret AS (IS:DOAS), which rose 10.00% or 17.30 points to trade at 190.30 at the close. Meanwhile, Fenerbahce Futbol AS (IS:FENER) added 9.95% or 4.78 points to end at 52.80 and  Akbank  TAS (IS:AKBNK) was up 8.47% or 5.70 points to 73.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Margun Enerji Uretim Sanayi  ve Ticaret AS (IS:MAGEN), which fell 4.08% or 1.06 points to trade at 24.90 at the close. Oyak Cimento Fabrikalari AS (IS:OYAKC) declined 3.60% or 1.00 points to end at 26.80 and Anadolu Anonim Turk Sigorta Sti (IS:ANSGR) was down 2.61% or 2.80 points to 104.40.
