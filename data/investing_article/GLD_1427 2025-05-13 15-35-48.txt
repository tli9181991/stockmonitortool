Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Tuesday, as losses in the Leasing & Factoring, Technology and Tourism sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 lost 0.48%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Dogus Otomotiv  Servis ve Ticaret AS (IS:DOAS), which rose 9.99% or 21.40 points to trade at 235.60 at the close. The worst performers of the session were ENKA Insaat ve Sanayi AS (IS:ENKAI), which fell 7.82% or 5.85 points to trade at 69.00 at the close.  Selcuk Ecza Deposu  Ticaret ve Sanayi AS (IS:SELEC) declined 6.87% or 4.80 points to end at 65.05 and  Celebi  Hava Servisi AS (IS:CLEBI) was down 6.73% or 182.50 points to 2,530.00. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 315 to 256 and 19 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.84% or 27.16 to $3,255.16 a troy ounce.
