Investing.com – Spain stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Telecoms & IT, Consumer Services and Financial Services & Real Estate sectors led shares higher. At the close in Madrid, the IBEX 35 rose 0.95%. The best performers of the session on the IBEX 35 were Repsol (BME:REP), which rose 2.86% or 0.33 points to trade at 11.87 at the close. Meanwhile, SOLARIA ENERGIA Y MEDIO AMBIENTE (BME:SLRS) added 2.72% or 0.20 points to end at 7.55 and  Amadeus  IT (BME:AMA) was up 2.47% or 1.72 points to 71.32 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Fluidra (BME:FLUI), which fell 1.83% or 0.40 points to trade at 21.50 at the close. ACS Actividades de Construccion y Servicios SA (BME:ACS) declined 1.19% or 0.65 points to end at 53.85 and ArcelorMittal SA (BME:MTS) was down 0.71% or 0.21 points to 29.39. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Madrid Stock Exchange by 150 to 44 and 22 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.11% or 3.40 to $3,004.50 a troy ounce.
