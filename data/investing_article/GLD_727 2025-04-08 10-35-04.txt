Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Oil & Gas, Real Estate and Consumer Durables sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 gained 1.69%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index added 1.49%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF), which rose 5.05% or 31.00 points to trade at 645.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Bharat Petroleum  Corp. Ltd. (NSE:BPCL) added 4.15% or 11.35 points to end at 285.05 and  Bharat Electronics  Ltd (NSE:BAJE) was up 3.68% or 10.00 points to 282.10 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (NSE:PGRD), which fell 0.09% or 0.25 points to trade at 289.15 at the close.  Trent  Ltd (NSE:TREN) added 0.40% or 19.15 points to end at 4,760.10 and  Tata Steel  Ltd (NSE:TISC) was up 0.44% or 0.57 points to 130.05. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Titan Company Ltd (BO:TITN) which rose 3.25% to 3,121.90, Bajaj Finance Ltd (BO:BJFN) which was up 3.21% to settle at 8,842.15 and Larsen & Toubro Ltd (BO:LART) which gained 3.07% to close at 3,165.00. The worst performers were Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (BO:PGRD) which was down 0.14% to 288.95 in late trade, Tata Steel Ltd (BO:TISC) which gained 0.39% to settle at 130.10 and Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. (BO:TCS) which was up 0.53% to 3,294.00 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 2008 to 365 and 27 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2959 rose and 849 declined, while 121 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 10.73% to 20.35. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.63% or 48.51 to $3,022.11 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 0.38% or 0.23 to hit $60.93 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract rose 0.28% or 0.18 to trade at $64.39 a barrel.
