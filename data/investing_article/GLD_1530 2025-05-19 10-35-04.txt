Investing.com – India stocks were lower after the close on Monday, as losses in the Technology, Fast Moving Consumer Goods and Healthcare sectors led shares lower. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 lost 0.30%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index declined 0.33%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  Bajaj Auto  Ltd (NSE:BAJA), which rose 4.12% or 349.50 points to trade at 8,832.00 at the close. Meanwhile, Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) added 1.85% or 12.30 points to end at 677.80 and Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (NSE:PGRD) was up 1.35% or 4.05 points to 304.25 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Grasim Industries  Ltd (NSE:GRAS), which fell 2.92% or 82.00 points to trade at 2,722.20 at the close.  Infosys  Ltd (NSE:INFY) declined 2.01% or 32.00 points to end at 1,557.90 and Tata Consumer Products Ltd (NSE:TACN) was down 1.54% or 18.00 points to 1,150.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (BO:PGRD) which rose 1.27% to 304.10, Bajaj Finance Ltd (BO:BJFN) which was up 0.91% to settle at 9,250.00 and NTPC Ltd (BO:NTPC) which gained 0.64% to close at 345.35. The worst performers were Eternal Ltd (BO:ETEA) which was down 2.99% to 238.40 in late trade, Infosys Ltd (BO:INFY) which lost 1.95% to settle at 1,558.70 and Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. (BO:TCS) which was down 1.20% to 3,520.30 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1521 to 888 and 39 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2433 rose and 1500 declined, while 174 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 4.26% to 17.25. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.75% or 55.91 to $3,243.21 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in July fell 1.36% or 0.84 to hit $61.13 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.28% or 0.84 to trade at $64.57 a barrel.
