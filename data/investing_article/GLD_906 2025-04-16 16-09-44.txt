Investing.com – Portugal stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Utilities, Consumer Services and Basic Materials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Lisbon, the PSI gained 0.59%. The best performers of the session on the PSI were  EDP Renovaveis  (ELI:EDPR), which rose 2.74% or 0.21 points to trade at 7.68 at the close. The worst performers of the session were Banco Comercial Portugues (ELI:BCP), which fell 4.04% or 0.02 points to trade at 0.55 at the close.  Corticeira Amorim  (ELI:CORA) declined 0.94% or 0.07 points to end at 7.34 and Nos SGPS SA (ELI:NOS) was down 0.12% or 0.01 points to 4.10.
