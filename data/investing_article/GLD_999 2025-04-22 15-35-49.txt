Investing.com – Turkey stocks were lower after the close on Tuesday, as losses in the Insurance, Information Technology and Electricity sectors led shares lower. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 fell 0.10%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Tekfen Holding  AS (IS:TKFEN), which rose 5.18% or 7.20 points to trade at 146.10 at the close. Meanwhile,  Pasifik Eurasia Lojistik Dis  Ticaret AS (IS:PASEU) added 4.24% or 2.50 points to end at 61.50 and Turk Telekomunikasyon AS (IS:TTKOM) was up 4.15% or 2.20 points to 55.20 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Isiklar Enerji ve Yapi Holding AS (IS:IEYHO), which fell 7.54% or 0.97 points to trade at 11.90 at the close. Grainturk Holding AS (IS:GRTHO) declined 5.46% or 19.00 points to end at 329.00 and Anadolu Hayat Emeklilik AS (IS:ANHYT) was down 5.06% or 4.65 points to 87.30. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 421 to 164 and 9 ended unchanged.
