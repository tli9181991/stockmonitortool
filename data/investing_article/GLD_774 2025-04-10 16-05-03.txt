Investing.com – Finland stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Technology, Financials and Telecoms sectors led shares higher. Kesko  Oyj (HE:KESKOB) added 1.37% or 0.25 points to end at 18.44 and  Stora Enso  Oyj R (HE:STERV) was up 1.52% or 0.11 points to 7.48.
