Investing.com – Canada stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Industrials, Materials and Telecoms sectors led shares lower. At the close in Toronto, the S&P/TSX Composite lost 0.07%. Meanwhile,  Spin Master Corp  (TSX:TOY) added 3.12% or 0.73 points to end at 24.14 and  TransAlta  Corp (TSX:TA) was up 2.38% or 0.29 points to 12.47 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were NovaGold Resources Inc (TSX:NG), which fell 10.74% or 0.74 points to trade at 6.15 at the close.  Energy Fuels Inc.  (TSX:EFR) declined 6.00% or 0.40 points to end at 6.27 and TFI International Inc (TSX:TFII) was down 5.52% or 6.46 points to 110.48. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Toronto Stock Exchange by 476 to 419 and 102 ended unchanged.
