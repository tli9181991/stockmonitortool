Investing.com – Morocco stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Pharmaceutical Industry, Software & Computer Services and Telecoms sectors led shares higher. At the close in Casablanca, the Moroccan All Shares rose 0.41%. The best performers of the session on the Moroccan All Shares were Stokvis Nord Afrique (CSE:SNA), which rose 9.96% or 2.08 points to trade at 22.97 at the close. Meanwhile, SMI (CSE:SMI) added 6.94% or 146.00 points to end at 2,250.00 and  Risma  (CSE:RIS) was up 6.07% or 17.00 points to 297.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were M2M Group (CSE:M2M), which fell 6.21% or 35.00 points to trade at 529.00 at the close.  Colorado  (CSE:COL) declined 3.33% or 1.98 points to end at 57.50 and Delta Holding S.A (CSE:DHO) was down 2.91% or 1.95 points to 65.05. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Casablanca Stock Exchange by 28 to 23 and 7 ended unchanged. Shares in Stokvis Nord Afrique (CSE:SNA) rose to 5-year highs; up 9.96% or 2.08 to 22.97.
