Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Real Estate, Power and Capital Goods sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 gained 0.34%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index added 0.20%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF), which rose 3.99% or 25.65 points to trade at 667.95 at the close. Meanwhile, HDFC Life Insurance Company Ltd (NSE:HDFL) added 3.75% or 24.00 points to end at 664.55 and  Apollo Hospitals  Enterprises Ltd. (NSE:APLH) was up 2.89% or 180.75 points to 6,427.55 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Tech Mahindra  Ltd (NSE:TEML), which fell 2.41% or 34.50 points to trade at 1,396.15 at the close.  Tata Consultancy Services  Ltd. (NSE:TCS) declined 1.56% or 55.35 points to end at 3,497.10 and  ITC  Ltd (NSE:ITC) was down 1.48% or 6.05 points to 403.05. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Tata Steel Ltd (BO:TISC) which rose 2.49% to 158.50, Zomato Ltd (BO:ZOMT) which was up 2.45% to settle at 223.70 and Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (BO:PGRD) which gained 2.22% to close at 276.80. The worst performers were Tech Mahindra Ltd (BO:TEML) which was down 2.28% to 1,398.45 in late trade, ITC Ltd (BO:ITC) which lost 1.51% to settle at 403.20 and Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. (BO:TCS) which was down 1.34% to 3,505.00 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1946 to 390 and 110 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2870 rose and 1014 declined, while 117 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was up 0.38% to 13.26. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.09% or 2.80 to $3,043.60 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May fell 0.36% or 0.24 to hit $66.51 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract fell 0.34% or 0.24 to trade at $70.32 a barrel.
