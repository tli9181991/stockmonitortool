Investing.com – Norway stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Healthcare Equipment & Services, Pharma Biotech & Life Sciences and Utilities sectors led shares higher. At the close in Oslo, the Oslo OBX added 0.20%. The best performers of the session on the Oslo OBX were  Subsea  7 SA (OL:SUBC), which rose 2.93% or 4.30 points to trade at 151.30 at the close. Meanwhile,  Kongsberg Gruppen ASA  (OL:KOG) added 2.92% or 44.00 points to end at 1,551.00 and  Nordic Semiconductor ASA  (OL:NOD) was up 2.69% or 3.10 points to 118.40 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Nel ASA (OL:NEL), which fell 4.54% or 0.11 points to trade at 2.35 at the close.  Tomra Systems  ASA (OL:TOM) declined 1.89% or 2.90 points to end at 150.50 and  Gjensidige Forsikring ASA  (OL:GJFG) was down 1.78% or 4.40 points to 242.80.
