Germany stocks mixed at close of trade; DAX up 0.34%
Investing.com – Germany stocks were mixed after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Technology, Telecoms and Food & Beverages sectors led shares higher while losses in the Construction, Retail and Media sectors led shares lower.
