Investing.com – Morocco stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Banking, Beverage and Transport sectors led shares higher. At the close in Casablanca, the Moroccan All Shares added 0.74% to hit a new 1-month high. The best performers of the session on the Moroccan All Shares were Label Vie (CSE:LBV), which rose 6.47% or 278.00 points to trade at 4,578.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Atlanta  (CSE:ATL) added 5.11% or 6.90 points to end at 141.90 and  CDM  (CSE:CDM) was up 4.56% or 48.00 points to 1,100.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Ste de Travaux de Realisation d’Ouvrages et de Constuction Industielle SA (CSE:STR), which fell 6.27% or 3.68 points to trade at 55.02 at the close. M2M Group (CSE:M2M) declined 4.75% or 26.00 points to end at 521.00 and Disty Technologies SA (CSE:DYT) was down 2.36% or 7.90 points to 327.10.
