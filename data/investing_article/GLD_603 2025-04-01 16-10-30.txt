Investing.com – Sweden stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Healthcare, Telecoms and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Stockholm, the OMX Stockholm 30 rose 0.63%. The best performers of the session on the OMX Stockholm 30 were Getinge AB ser. B (ST:GETIb), which rose 3.15% or 6.80 points to trade at 222.60 at the close. Meanwhile, SAAB AB ser. B (ST:SAABb) added 2.57% or 10.10 points to end at 403.80 and Samhallsbyggnadsbolaget i Norden AB (ST:SBBb) was up 2.37% or 0.08 points to 3.46 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were NIBE Industrier AB ser. B (ST:NIBEb), which fell 1.63% or 0.60 points to trade at 37.30 at the close. Kinnevik, Investment AB ser. B (ST:KINVb) declined 0.75% or 0.50 points to end at 69.90 and ASSA ABLOY AB ser. B (ST:ASSAb) was down 0.53% or 1.60 points to 298.30. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Stockholm Stock Exchange by 484 to 267 and 57 ended unchanged. Shares in NIBE Industrier AB ser. B (ST:NIBEb) fell to 3-years lows; falling 1.63% or 0.60 to 37.30. Shares in Kinnevik, Investment AB ser. B (ST:KINVb) fell to 5-year lows; losing 0.75% or 0.50 to 69.90.
