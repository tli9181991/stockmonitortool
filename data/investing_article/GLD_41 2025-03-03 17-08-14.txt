Investing.com – Italy stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Industrials, Travel & Leisure and Financials sectors led shares higher. At the close in Milan, the Investing.com Italy 40 rose 1.07%. The best performers of the session on the Investing.com Italy 40 were Leonardo SpA (BIT:LDOF), which rose 16.13% or 6.22 points to trade at 44.78 at the close. Meanwhile, Iveco Group NV (BIT:IVG) added 6.15% or 0.93 points to end at 16.05 and UniCredit SpA (BIT:CRDI) was up 2.69% or 1.36 points to 51.97 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Amplifon  (BIT:AMPF), which fell 2.46% or 0.60 points to trade at 23.79 at the close.  Saipem  SpA (BIT:SPMI) declined 2.18% or 0.05 points to end at 2.15 and  Prysmian  SpA (BIT:PRY) was down 1.51% or 0.86 points to 56.18.
