Investing.com – Brazil stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Financials, Electric Power and Real Estate sectors led shares higher. At the close in Sao Paulo, the Bovespa gained 0.21% to hit a new 6-months high. The worst performers of the session were  IRB Brasil Resseguros  SA (BVMF:IRBR3), which fell 5.37% or 2.58 points to trade at 45.50 at the close.
