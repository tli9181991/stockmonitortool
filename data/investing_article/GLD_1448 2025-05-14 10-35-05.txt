Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Metals, Real Estate and Capital Goods sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 rose 0.36%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index gained 0.22%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  Tata Steel  Ltd (NSE:TISC), which rose 3.96% or 5.92 points to trade at 155.35 at the close. Meanwhile, Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) added 2.75% or 17.40 points to end at 651.00 and  Bharat Electronics  Ltd (NSE:BAJE) was up 2.56% or 8.60 points to 344.35 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Asian Paints  Ltd. (NSE:ASPN), which fell 1.66% or 38.60 points to trade at 2,286.00 at the close.  Cipla  Ltd. (NSE:CIPL) declined 1.33% or 20.20 points to end at 1,499.90 and  Kotak Mahindra Bank  Ltd. (NSE:KTKM) was down 1.16% or 24.60 points to 2,091.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Tata Steel Ltd (BO:TISC) which rose 3.95% to 155.40, Tech Mahindra Ltd (BO:TEML) which was up 2.26% to settle at 1,608.10 and Eternal Ltd (BO:ETEA) which gained 2.20% to close at 236.85. The worst performers were Asian Paints Ltd. (BO:ASPN) which was down 1.64% to 2,287.00 in late trade, Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. (BO:KTKM) which lost 1.08% to settle at 2,092.95 and Tata Motors Ltd (BO:TAMO) which was down 1.02% to 700.70 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1842 to 539 and 37 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2739 rose and 1089 declined, while 134 ended unchanged. Shares in Bharat Electronics Ltd (NSE:BAJE) rose to all time highs; rising 2.56% or 8.60 to 344.35.  The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 5.48% to 17.20. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 0.37% or 12.11 to $3,235.69 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 1.12% or 0.71 to hit $62.96 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.07% or 0.71 to trade at $65.92 a barrel. USD/INR was up 0.02% to 85.26, while EUR/INR rose 0.48% to 95.87. The US Dollar Index Futures was down 0.51% at 100.32.
