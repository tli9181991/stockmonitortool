Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Telecoms, Metal Products & Machinery and Banking sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 added 3.80% to hit a new 1-month high. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Celebi  Hava Servisi AS (IS:CLEBI), which rose 10.00% or 246.50 points to trade at 2,712.50 at the close. Meanwhile,  Anadolu Efes  Biracilik ve Malt Sanayi AS (IS:AEFES) added 9.99% or 15.20 points to end at 167.30 and ENKA Insaat ve Sanayi AS (IS:ENKAI) was up 9.99% or 6.80 points to 74.85 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Aselsan  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS), which fell 5.90% or 8.80 points to trade at 140.40 at the close. Koza Anadolu Metal Madencilik Isletmeleri AS (IS:KOZAA) declined 4.35% or 3.80 points to end at 83.55 and Ral Yatirim Holding AS (IS:RALYH) was down 4.21% or 5.20 points to 118.30. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 444 to 137 and 10 ended unchanged. Shares in ENKA Insaat ve Sanayi AS (IS:ENKAI) rose to all time highs; up 9.99% or 6.80 to 74.85.  Gold Futures for June delivery was down 3.07% or 102.75 to $3,241.25 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June rose 2.21% or 1.35 to hit $62.37 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract rose 2.05% or 1.31 to trade at $65.22 a barrel.
