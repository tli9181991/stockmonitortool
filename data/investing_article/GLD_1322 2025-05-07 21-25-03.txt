Investing.com – Russia stocks were higher after the close on Wednesday, as gains in the Power, Oil & Gas and Telecoms sectors led shares higher. At the close in Moscow, the MOEX Russia Index rose 0.52%. The best performers of the session on the MOEX Russia Index were  PhosAgro  PJSC (MCX:PHOR), which rose 4.88% or 291.00 points to trade at 6,254.00 at the close. The worst performers of the session were Polyus PJSC (MCX:PLZL), which fell 2.26% or 40.80 points to trade at 1,763.40 at the close. Magnitogorskiy Metallurgicheskiy Kombinat PAO (MCX:MAGN) declined 2.10% or 0.67 points to end at 31.00 and ADS Ozon Holdings PLC ORD SHS (MCX:OZONDR) was down 0.78% or 28.50 points to 3,614.50. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Moscow Stock Exchange by 138 to 95 and 19 ended unchanged.
