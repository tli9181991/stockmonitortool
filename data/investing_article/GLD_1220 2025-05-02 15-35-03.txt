Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Wholesale & Retail Trade, Transport and Basic Metals sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 gained 0.98%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were Isiklar Enerji ve Yapi Holding AS (IS:IEYHO), which rose 9.91% or 1.03 points to trade at 11.42 at the close. Meanwhile,  Katilimevim Tasarruf Finansman AS  (IS:KTLEV) added 9.74% or 7.45 points to end at 83.90 and  Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret  AS (IS:OBAMS) was up 9.14% or 6.20 points to 74.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Borusan Yatirim ve Pazarlama AS (IS:BRYAT), which fell 4.25% or 90.00 points to trade at 2,030.00 at the close.  Limak Dogu Anadolu Cimento Sanayi  ve Ticaret AS (IS:LMKDC) declined 4.11% or 1.14 points to end at 26.62 and  Aselsan  Elektronik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:ASELS) was down 3.85% or 5.20 points to 129.80. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 403 to 168 and 20 ended unchanged. Shares in Oba Makarnacilik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS (IS:OBAMS) rose to all time highs; up 9.14% or 6.20 to 74.00.  Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.84% or 27.05 to $3,249.25 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in June fell 1.74% or 1.03 to hit $58.21 a barrel, while the July Brent oil contract fell 1.46% or 0.91 to trade at $61.22 a barrel.
