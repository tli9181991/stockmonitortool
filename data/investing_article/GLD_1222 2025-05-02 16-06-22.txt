Investing.com – France stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Industrials, Basic Materials and Technology sectors led shares higher. At the close in Paris, the CAC 40 gained 2.33%, while the SBF 120 index added 2.23%. The best performers of the session on the CAC 40 were Schneider Electric SE (EPA:SCHN), which rose 5.68% or 11.60 points to trade at 215.80 at the close. Meanwhile, Airbus Group SE (EPA:AIR) added 5.31% or 7.82 points to end at 154.96 and ArcelorMittal SA (AS:MT) was up 4.48% or 1.16 points to 27.08 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Teleperformance  SE (EPA:TEPRF), which fell 7.28% or 7.02 points to trade at 89.44 at the close.  Edenred  SA (EPA:EDEN) declined 1.39% or 0.38 points to end at 27.00 and Orange SA (EPA:ORAN) was down 0.86% or 0.11 points to 12.68. The top performers on the SBF 120 were Eutelsat Communications SA (EPA:ETL) which rose 12.20% to 4.00, Emeis SA (EPA:EMEIS) which was up 8.49% to settle at 12.25 and  Ubisoft  Entertainment (EPA:UBIP) which gained 7.29% to close at 11.12. The worst performers were Teleperformance SE (EPA:TEPRF) which was down 7.28% to 89.44 in late trade, Television Francaise 1 SA (EPA:TFFP) which lost 4.35% to settle at 8.04 and SES (EPA:SESFd) which was down 2.49% to 4.54 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Paris Stock Exchange by 344 to 166 and 64 ended unchanged. The CAC 40 VIX, which measures the implied volatility of CAC 40 options, was unchanged 0.00% to 18.96 a new 52-week high. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.45% or 14.64 to $3,236.84 a troy ounce.
