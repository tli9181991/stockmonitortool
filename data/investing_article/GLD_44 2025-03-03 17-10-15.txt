Investing.com – Spain stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Financial Services & Real Estate, Telecoms & IT and Building & Construction sectors led shares higher. At the close in Madrid, the IBEX 35 rose 0.31% to hit a new 5-year high. The best performers of the session on the IBEX 35 were Indra A (BME:IDR), which rose 9.83% or 2.06 points to trade at 23.02 at the close. Meanwhile, ArcelorMittal SA (BME:MTS) added 4.84% or 1.33 points to end at 28.79 and  Grifols  SA (BME:GRLS) was up 3.58% or 0.38 points to 11.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Merlin Properties SA  (BME:MRL), which fell 2.78% or 0.29 points to trade at 10.16 at the close. Redeia Corporacion SA (BME:REDE) declined 1.74% or 0.30 points to end at 16.98 and Industria de Diseno Textil SA (BME:ITX) was down 1.62% or 0.84 points to 51.16. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Madrid Stock Exchange by 124 to 73 and 17 ended unchanged. Shares in Indra A (BME:IDR) rose to all time highs; up 9.83% or 2.06 to 23.02.
