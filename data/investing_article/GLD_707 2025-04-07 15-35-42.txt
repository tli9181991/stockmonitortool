Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Monday, as gains in the Wholesale & Retail Trade, Transport and Services sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 rose 0.29%. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Tekfen Holding  AS (IS:TKFEN), which rose 9.75% or 12.70 points to trade at 143.00 at the close. Meanwhile,  Sekerbank  TAS (IS:SKBNK) added 8.70% or 0.44 points to end at 5.50 and  Pasifik Eurasia Lojistik Dis  Ticaret AS (IS:PASEU) was up 6.96% or 2.66 points to 40.88 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Koza Anadolu Metal Madencilik Isletmeleri AS (IS:KOZAA), which fell 5.67% or 4.80 points to trade at 79.80 at the close. Kardemir Karabuk Demir Celik Sanayi ve Ticaret AS Class D (IS:KRDMD) declined 4.26% or 1.14 points to end at 25.62 and Borusan Birlesik Boru Fabrikalari Sanayi Ve Ticaret AS (IS:BRSAN) was down 3.49% or 14.00 points to 387.00. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 352 to 220 and 19 ended unchanged.
