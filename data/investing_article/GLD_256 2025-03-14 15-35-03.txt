Investing.com – Turkey stocks were higher after the close on Friday, as gains in the Food & Beverages, Non-Metal Mineral Products and Leasing & Factoring sectors led shares higher. At the close in Istanbul, the BIST 100 added 1.05% to hit a new 6-months high. The best performers of the session on the BIST 100 were  Tekfen Holding  AS (IS:TKFEN), which rose 10.00% or 8.80 points to trade at 96.80 at the close. Meanwhile,  LDR Turizm AS  (IS:LIDER) added 6.66% or 11.70 points to end at 187.30 and Coca-Cola Icecek AS (IS:CCOLA) was up 6.65% or 3.60 points to 57.70 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Altinay Savunma Teknolojileri AS  (IS:ALTNY), which fell 2.38% or 2.10 points to trade at 86.00 at the close.  Enerya Enerji AS  (IS:ENERY) declined 1.57% or 0.06 points to end at 3.76 and  Cvk Maden Isletmeleri Sanayi ve  Ticaret AS (IS:CVKMD) was down 1.35% or 0.15 points to 10.97. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Istanbul Stock Exchange by 406 to 159 and 27 ended unchanged.
