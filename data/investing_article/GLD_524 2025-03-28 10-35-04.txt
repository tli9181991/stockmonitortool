Investing.com – India stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Real Estate, Technology and Auto sectors led shares lower. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 fell 0.31%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index declined 0.25%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were Tata Consumer Products Ltd (NSE:TACN), which rose 2.91% or 28.35 points to trade at 1,001.90 at the close. Meanwhile,  Kotak Mahindra Bank  Ltd. (NSE:KTKM) added 2.00% or 42.55 points to end at 2,171.20 and  Britannia Industries  Ltd (NSE:BRIT) was up 1.98% or 95.70 points to 4,936.90 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Wipro Ltd  (NSE:WIPR), which fell 3.66% or 9.95 points to trade at 262.25 at the close.  IndusInd Bank  Ltd. (NSE:INBK) declined 3.52% or 23.70 points to end at 649.85 and Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) was down 3.32% or 22.50 points to 656.00. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. (BO:KTKM) which rose 1.78% to 2,169.10, Hindustan Unilever Ltd. (BO:HLL) which was up 0.95% to settle at 2,258.00 and Bharti Airtel Ltd (BO:BRTI) which gained 0.79% to close at 1,737.00. The worst performers were IndusInd Bank Ltd. (BO:INBK) which was down 3.50% to 650.00 in late trade, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. (BO:MAHM) which lost 2.58% to settle at 2,662.65 and Eternal Ltd (BO:ZOMT) which was down 2.21% to 201.20 at the close. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1482 to 822 and 146 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2414 fell and 1435 advanced, while 120 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 4.25% to 12.73. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.50% or 15.39 to $3,106.29 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May fell 0.14% or 0.10 to hit $69.82 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract fell 0.16% or 0.12 to trade at $73.22 a barrel. USD/INR was down 0.32% to 85.36, while EUR/INR fell 0.46% to 92.09. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.09% at 104.09.
