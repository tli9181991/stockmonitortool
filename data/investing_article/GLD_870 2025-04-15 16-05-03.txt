Investing.com – Belgium stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Industrials, Technology and Utilities sectors led shares higher. At the close in Brussels, the BEL 20 rose 1.47%. The best performers of the session on the BEL 20 were  Warehouses de Pauw  Comm VA (EBR:WDPP), which rose 4.85% or 1.00 points to trade at 21.60 at the close. Meanwhile,  Melexis  NV (EBR:MLXS) added 3.73% or 1.76 points to end at 48.92 and Elia (EBR:ELI) was up 3.56% or 3.05 points to 88.75 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Ageas  (EBR:AGES), which fell 2.45% or 1.30 points to trade at 51.70 at the close.  Galapagos  NV (AS:GLPG) declined 0.94% or 0.20 points to end at 21.14 and  Azelis  Corporate Services NV (EBR:AZE) was down 0.06% or 0.01 points to 15.54. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Brussels Stock Exchange by 67 to 31 and 12 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 0.39% or 12.50 to $3,238.80 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May fell 0.76% or 0.47 to hit $61.06 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract fell 0.79% or 0.51 to trade at $64.37 a barrel. EUR/USD was down 0.62% to 1.13, while EUR/GBP fell 0.86% to 0.85. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.49% at 99.88.
