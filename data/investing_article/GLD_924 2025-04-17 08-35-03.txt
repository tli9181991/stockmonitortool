Investing.com – Indonesia stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Infrastructure, Financials and Agriculture sectors led shares higher. At the close in Jakarta, the IDX Composite Index added 0.30%. The best performers of the session on the IDX Composite Index were  Wahana Inti MakmurTbk PT  (JK:NASI), which rose 34.43% or 21.00 points to trade at 82.00 at the close. Meanwhile, Centratama Telekomunikasi Ind (JK:CENT) added 31.46% or 28.00 points to end at 117.00 and Fortune Mate Indonesia Tbk (JK:FMII) was up 24.83% or 72.00 points to 362.00 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Himalaya Energi Perkasa Tbk PT (JK:HADE), which fell 14.29% or 1.00 points to trade at 6.00 at the close. Lion Metal Works Tbk (JK:LION) declined 11.72% or 56.00 points to end at 422.00 and  DMS Propertindo Tbk PT  (JK:KOTA) was down 12.50% or 1.00 points to 7.00. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Jakarta Stock Exchange by 322 to 301 and 223 ended unchanged.
