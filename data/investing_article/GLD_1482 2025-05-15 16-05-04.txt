Investing.com – Belgium stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Utilities, Telecoms and Consumer Services sectors led shares higher. At the close in Brussels, the BEL 20 added 0.33%. The best performers of the session on the BEL 20 were  Melexis  NV (EBR:MLXS), which rose 2.07% or 1.25 points to trade at 61.50 at the close. Meanwhile,  Cofinimmo  SA (EBR:COFB) added 1.78% or 1.35 points to end at 77.35 and  Argen-X  (EBR:ARGX) was up 1.78% or 8.50 points to 485.80 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Aedifica  (EBR:AOO), which fell 5.21% or 3.45 points to trade at 62.80 at the close.  Syensqo  SA (EBR:SYENS) declined 2.51% or 1.68 points to end at 65.16 and Ackermans & Van Haaren NV (EBR:ACKB) was down 1.22% or 2.80 points to 226.60. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Brussels Stock Exchange by 52 to 40 and 16 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for June delivery was up 1.01% or 32.29 to $3,220.59 a troy ounce.
