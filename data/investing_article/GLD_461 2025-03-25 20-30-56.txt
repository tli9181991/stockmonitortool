Investing.com – Canada stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Consumer Staples, Materials and Telecoms sectors led shares higher. At the close in Toronto, the S&P/TSX Composite gained 0.14%. The best performers of the session on the S&P/TSX Composite were  Torex Gold Resources Inc  (TSX:TXG), which rose 7.39% or 2.65 points to trade at 38.52 at the close. Meanwhile,  Sandstorm Gold Ltd . (TSX:SSL) added 7.13% or 0.70 points to end at 10.52 and Eldorado Gold Corp (TSX:ELD) was up 5.33% or 1.20 points to 23.70 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were BRP Inc (TSX:DOO), which fell 6.88% or 3.75 points to trade at 50.78 at the close. Nutrien Ltd (TSX:NTR) declined 4.81% or 3.62 points to end at 71.62 and NovaGold Resources Inc (TSX:NG) was down 4.73% or 0.22 points to 4.43. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Toronto Stock Exchange by 490 to 410 and 104 ended unchanged. Shares in Sandstorm Gold Ltd. (TSX:SSL) rose to 52-week highs; up 7.13% or 0.70 to 10.52.
