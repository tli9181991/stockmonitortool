Investing.com – India stocks were higher after the close on Tuesday, as gains in the Real Estate, Consumer Durables and Capital Goods sectors led shares higher. At the close in NSE, the Nifty 50 rose 1.51%, while the BSE Sensex 30 index gained 1.53%. The best performers of the session on the Nifty 50 were  ICICI Bank Ltd  (NSE:ICBK), which rose 3.22% or 40.85 points to trade at 1,309.85 at the close. Meanwhile,  Mahindra & Mahindra  Ltd. (NSE:MAHM) added 3.19% or 86.25 points to end at 2,791.40 and Shriram Finance Ltd. (NSE:SHMF) was up 3.06% or 19.05 points to 642.30 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Bajaj Finserv Ltd (NSE:BJFS), which fell 1.44% or 26.95 points to trade at 1,844.65 at the close.  Bharti Airtel  Ltd. (NSE:BRTI) declined 0.72% or 11.85 points to end at 1,627.30 and  Tech Mahindra  Ltd (NSE:TEML) was down 0.66% or 9.45 points to 1,430.65. The top performers on the BSE Sensex 30 were Zomato Ltd (BO:ZOMT) which rose 7.43% to 219.00, ICICI Bank Ltd (BO:ICBK) which was up 3.40% to settle at 1,311.45 and Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. (BO:MAHM) which gained 3.07% to close at 2,787.00. The worst performers were Bajaj Finserv Ltd (BO:BJFS) which was down 1.33% to 1,847.00 in late trade, Tech Mahindra Ltd (BO:TEML) which lost 0.45% to settle at 1,433.20 and Bharti Airtel Ltd (BO:BRTI) which was down 0.31% to 1,634.35 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the India National Stock Exchange by 1834 to 497 and 115 ended unchanged; on the Bombay Stock Exchange, 2686 rose and 1186 declined, while 122 ended unchanged. The India VIX, which measures the implied volatility of Nifty 50 options, was down 1.17% to 13.26 a new 1-month low. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 0.83% or 25.09 to $3,031.19 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 1.38% or 0.93 to hit $68.30 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract rose 1.34% or 0.95 to trade at $72.02 a barrel. USD/INR was down 0.08% to 86.62, while EUR/INR fell 0.07% to 94.63. The US Dollar Index Futures was unchanged 0.00% at 103.01.
