Investing.com – Spain stocks were higher after the close on Thursday, as gains in the Telecoms & IT, Chemical, Petroleum & Plastic and Building & Construction sectors led shares higher. At the close in Madrid, the IBEX 35 gained 0.08%. The best performers of the session on the IBEX 35 were  Telefonica  (BME:TEF), which rose 2.74% or 0.12 points to trade at 4.34 at the close. Meanwhile,  Acerinox  (BME:ACX) added 1.83% or 0.20 points to end at 11.13 and  Enagas  (BME:ENAG) was up 1.41% or 0.18 points to 12.93 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were Fluidra (BME:FLUI), which fell 1.43% or 0.32 points to trade at 22.00 at the close.  Puig Brands SA  (BME:PUIGb) declined 1.33% or 0.24 points to end at 17.45 and Banco de Sabadell SA (BME:SABE) was down 1.16% or 0.03 points to 2.65. Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Madrid Stock Exchange by 104 to 84 and 21 ended unchanged. Gold Futures for April delivery was up 1.50% or 44.34 to $2,991.14 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in April fell 1.42% or 0.96 to hit $66.72 a barrel, while the May Brent oil contract fell 1.30% or 0.92 to trade at $70.03 a barrel. EUR/USD was unchanged 0.19% to 1.09, while EUR/GBP unchanged 0.10% to 0.84. The US Dollar Index Futures was up 0.14% at 103.73.
