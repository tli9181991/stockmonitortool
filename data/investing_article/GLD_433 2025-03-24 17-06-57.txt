Investing.com – Germany stocks were mixed after the close on Monday, as gains in the Technology, Food & Beverages and Software sectors led shares higher while losses in the Construction, Financial Services and Telecoms sectors led shares lower. At the close in Frankfurt, the DAX fell 0.16%, while the MDAX index climbed 0.05%, and the TecDAX index added 0.69%. The best performers of the session on the DAX were Infineon Technologies AG NA O.N. (ETR:IFXGn), which rose 3.31% or 1.10 points to trade at 34.35 at the close. Meanwhile,  Deutsche Bank  AG NA O.N. (ETR:DBKGn) added 2.60% or 0.58 points to end at 22.86 and  Zalando SE  (ETR:ZALG) was up 1.81% or 0.56 points to 31.57 in late trade. The worst performers of the session were  Bayer AG  NA (ETR:BAYGN), which fell 6.94% or 1.67 points to trade at 22.41 at the close.  Beiersdorf  AG O.N. (ETR:BEIG) declined 4.17% or 5.30 points to end at 121.90 and Vonovia SE (ETR:VNAn) was down 3.77% or 0.96 points to 24.48. The top performers on the MDAX were Carl Zeiss Meditec AG (ETR:AFXG) which rose 5.16% to 67.25,  Evotec AG  O.N. (ETR:EVTG) which was up 4.98% to settle at 6.64 and  Aurubis AG  (ETR:NAFG) which gained 4.08% to close at 93.20. The worst performers were AUTO1 Group SE (ETR:AG1G) which was down 4.80% to 21.40 in late trade, Deutsche Wohnen AG (ETR:DWNG) which lost 3.63% to settle at 19.90 and  Hochtief  AG (ETR:HOTG) which was down 3.55% to 168.40 at the close. The top performers on the TecDAX were Carl Zeiss Meditec AG (ETR:AFXG) which rose 5.16% to 67.25, Evotec AG O.N. (ETR:EVTG) which was up 4.98% to settle at 6.64 and Infineon Technologies AG NA O.N. (ETR:IFXGn) which gained 3.31% to close at 34.35. The worst performers were  Nordex  SE O.N. (ETR:NDXG) which was down 2.64% to 15.48 in late trade,  PNE Wind AG  (ETR:PNEGn) which lost 1.53% to settle at 14.18 and Deutsche Telekom AG Na (ETR:DTEGn) which was down 1.36% to 33.30 at the close. Rising stocks outnumbered declining ones on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange by 351 to 289 and 25 ended unchanged. Shares in Vonovia SE (ETR:VNAn) fell to 52-week lows; losing 3.77% or 0.96 to 24.48.  The DAX volatility index, which measures the implied volatility of DAX options, was up 2.83% to 22.81 a new 6-months high. Gold Futures for June delivery was down 0.15% or 4.50 to $3,043.90 a troy ounce. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Crude oil for delivery in May rose 1.08% or 0.74 to hit $69.02 a barrel, while the June Brent oil contract rose 0.96% or 0.69 to trade at $72.30 a barrel.
